### My Ideas (Doesn't conform to a Theme)
**The Death March** Combat 30%, Exploration 60%, Social Interaction 10%
The party are all felons, sentenced to die by the Holy Kingdom of Halmyr for their crimes regardless of whether they did their deeds or not. Rather than execute these miscreants themselves, they've been sent to the Badlands, the lands overrun by Demons, where if the gods grace them, they might close the Darkrift for good and be exonerated of their crimes.

**Blood For Blood** Combat 40%, Exploration 40%, Social Interaction 20%
The Party are assassins, raised by the Kingdom of Valdyr in order to kill high ranking officials of Korvia, and if able, the Emperor of Korvia himself in order to destabilise the nation.

**Keepers of Peace** Combat 34%, Exploration 33%, Social Interaction 33%
The party are new members of the Wayfarer Guild in Korvia, and must travel the 5 main regions, helping out the people while following the trails of a strange occurrences, which they will eventually uncover to be a threat to the entire nation.

## Theme Ideas
### Brought Together by Fate
**The Road to Kraggath** Combat 60%, Exploration 20%, Social Interaction 20%
Strangers sharing passage on a trade wagon into Ardia find themselves ambushed by Orcish mercenaries gone rogue. With the driver dead, they must make their way to the Ardian port town and survive long enough to learn why someone wants the cargo destroyed.

**Gates of the Eldenwood** Combat 35%, Exploration 55%, Social Interaction 10%
Separated from their respective groups by a sudden storm near the Dunvaar-Myrathin border, a band of strangers must navigate the ancient Eldenwood and find their way back to civilization. The forest is older than memory, and things in it have started behaving strangely.

**The Wraithport Inquisition** Combat 25%, Exploration 15%, Social Interaction 60%
Passing through the Fourfold Dominion's capital, the characters are all separately accused of the same crime by the same witness. To clear their names they must navigate pirate court, rival captains with competing motives, and a Sea Lord who wants the case buried quickly.

### Guardians
**Escort Through the Badlands** Combat 50%, Exploration 40%, Social Interaction 10%
A Halmyran scholar has discovered a means of weakening the Darkrift from inside the Gates of Ikanos. A suicide mission, should it fail. She will not survive to see her goal completed, successful or not without protection. The party must escort her through the corrupted Badlands while fending off demonic forces.

**The Frozen Heir** Combat 30%, Exploration 50%, Social Interaction 20%
High Chieftain of the Free Tribes, Valdris has a child that he has kept secret. With the High Chieftain's health failing, a trusted few are tasked with locating and safely retrieving his heir from a remote settlement deep in the Frozen North before rival tribal chieftains learn the child exists and have the child killed.

**The Valeniiri Envoy** Combat 25%, Exploration 15%, Social Interaction 60%
Valeniir has sent its first official diplomatic envoy to the mainland in centuries. The party is assigned as the escort and liaison team. Multiple factions want to exploit or obstruct the mission, and keeping the envoy alive means navigating aggressors, bribes, and competing agendas without causing a major incident.

### Mercenaries
**Debt Collection** Combat 40%, Exploration 20%, Social Interaction 40%
A merchant guild operating out of Korvia has hired the party to recover a substantial debt from a mid-ranking captain in the Fourfold Dominion who has gone quiet on the Emerald Isles. When they arrive in Wraithport, the captain is dead and the coin is gone. The guild doesn't care about the circumstances; they want what they're owed, or they want answers they can sell. Navigating Dominion politics and rowdy pirates without a fleet at your back is a delicate business.

**The Obellian Survey** Combat 30%, Exploration 50%, Social Interaction 20%
A Karrethic mage consortium has hired the party to survey a newly discovered ruin beneath the Shifting Sands believed to be Obellian in origin. The structure descends further than any on record, with each level more intact and more dangerous than the last.

**A Favour for the Pirate King** Combat 40%, Exploration 10%, Social Interaction 50%
The King of Pirates, Amir Al'Bahir needs a debt collected from a Korvian noble, and doing it himself would mean war. The party is hired to recover it.

### Heroes of the Resistance
**The Korvian Underground** Combat 60%, Exploration 25%, Social Interaction 15%
A network of Beastfolk slaves in Korvia are planning an uprising. The party operates as a strike cell, dismantling the infrastructure of slave-keeping one holding at a time, sabotaging the holds and freeing prisoners before the Imperial Guard can respond in force.

**On the Run** Combat 30%, Exploration 50%, Social Interaction 20%
The Ministry of Prosecution has labelled the party heretics. With every road watched, they must escape Halmyr entirely by crossing the Crescent Range into Myrathin through a route no-one has used since the Age of Darkness. The mountains have not been empty since then either.

**The Tribal Accord** Combat 20%, Exploration 20%, Social Interaction 60%
High Chieftain Valdris cannot leave the camps of the Free Tribes. So the party travels on his behalf across the Frozen North to bring three holdout chieftains into the coalition.

### Revolutionaries
**Fire in Eikel** Combat 55%, Exploration 20%, Social Interaction 25%
A new generation of Myrathi extremists plan to recreate the Eikel Massacre that kickstarted the revolution a hundred years ago. The party works to stop the plot, operating without official sanction in a city where the local garrison cannot be trusted.

**Gunrunners** Combat 20%, Exploration 55%, Social Interaction 25%
Word has leaked that a Dunvaaran hold has been secretly occupied by Korvian intelligence operatives seeking access to the Commonwealth's sealed tech archives. The party are said operatives who have infiltrated the hold through unmapped tunnels. They must find the sealed tech, retrieve it and get out before the Commonwealth discovers them and seals the hold permanently.

**Ardia Unchained** Combat 15%, Exploration 25%, Social Interaction 60%
A movement is forming among Ardia's Orcs to formally petition the continent for recognition of their sovereignty and break Halmyr's colonial grip on the land. The party works as their political operators, building relationships with allied nations, neutralising Halmyran informants, and keeping the movement's more volatile factions from destroying it from within.

### Seekers
**The Labyrinth of Tyr** Combat 50%, Exploration 40%, Social Interaction 10%
Fragments of a map have been discovered by the party, that tells of a grand treasure that awaits them the heart of a labyrinth. However, it's a race against time as word spreads that multiple fragments of this map have been found.

**The Lost Capital** Combat 30%, Exploration 60%, Social Interaction 10%
Fragments of a Marqiri map have surfaced pointing to the buried location of Dahrim, the lost capital beneath the Shifting Sands. A Karrethic sage believes the city's archive contains the original ritual used to lift Karreth into the sky. The party must navigate the living desert to find it before the sands shift again.

**The Missing Pope** Combat 15%, Exploration 20%, Social Interaction 65%
Rumours have surfaced of the location of the previous Pope of the Octavian Faith who went missing some years ago. The party must trace these rumours, question factions, determine who can be trusted, and find the Pope.
