### Brought Together by Fate
You find yourselves working together because of the circumstances. Despite this being a common trope in classic fantasy tales, it is actually the most difficult to pull off since your group will lack a precise goal: you must work with each other during play to build a web of relationships.

If you choose this premise, it may be a good idea to switch to a different type of group theme once your characters have developed a common goal.

+ What emotional or familial connections exist between your characters?
+ Have your characters met in the past? Are there any tensions or rivalries?
+ What do you have in common? Beliefs, enemies, past experiences?

### Guardians
Your player characters have been chosen, or tasked with a mission that could change the very fate of the world. They could be sworn to protect an item like an artifact, or a particular NPC, such as the Chosen One destined to seal away a great evil, a Young Prince that must reunite his kingdom following his father's assassination, a Holy Saintess who must cleanse the land of evil beings.

+ Who or what are you guarding? What bonds, ties or rivalries exist between each member of the group?
+ What is your mission? Who gave it to you? Do the authorities approve of it?
+ Why does this thing or person need protection? Who or what threatens the mission?

### Mercenaries
Similar to the Guardians theme but slightly different, your player characters are a part of a band of mercenaries that have been hired, and tasked with a mission that could change the very fate of the world. Your mission could be to assassinate a political leader, join and fight in a war between rival kingdoms, or even infiltrate a cult.

+ What is your mission? What bonds, ties rivalries exists between each member of your mercenary band?
+ Who hired you for this mission?
+ Why have you specifically been hired?

### Heroes of the Resistance
You are the last line of defence against a terrible power, a darkness that threatens to envelop the known world. Such as a power-hungry kingdom or an oppressive empire. The fate of many lies within your hands.

+ Why is this daunting task up to you? Have you fought against this enemy before?
+ Which factions helped you, and which ones betrayed or opposed you?
+ Who are the most infamous among the enemy leaders and followers?

### Revolutionaries
You form a small force that fights tooth and nail against a dominant power: it might be a tyrannical government, a military occupation, or even a ruthless company. Society and security forces probably consider you criminals or terrorists.

+ What is the price your character paid for their insubordination?
+ Which factions promised you their help? Which factions might oppose you?
+ What are your enemies’ crimes, and why is no one else opposing them?

### Seekers
You are desperately searching for someone or something. It could be a political figure, an ancient relic, a secret or hidden location only known to a few, or even a legendary hoard of treasure. What you seek is important to you, or it could be the world’s last hope.

+ What is it you are trying to to find?
+ What do you know of your mission? Who or what gave you this information?
+ Who doesn't want you to find what you seek?
+ What prompted each of you to join this mission?
