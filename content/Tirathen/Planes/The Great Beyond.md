The Great Beyond is a term that encompasses all the planes of existence that make up the known multiverse. These realities beyond our reality are the domains of the gods, and play home to non-godly powers fit for worship, planar dragons, and entirely alien species.

There are nineteen major planes that comprise the Great Beyond, modelled as two spheres: the Inner Sphere and the Outer Sphere. Connecting the two spheres is the [[Astral Plane]]. The spaces between the primary nineteen planes contain countless demiplanes. Despite the names implying a spherical layout, the planes exist on dimensions incomprehensible to mortals, thus these terms are used to best represent the planes in two or three dimensions.

## The Inner Sphere
The Inner Sphere is the centre of the Great Beyond, sitting at the hub of creation surrounded by the Astral Plane, and is the source of souls and origin of quintessence. It is composed of the immensely huge, yet finite layers of the Elemental Planes, which surround the [[Creation's Forge]], the [[First World]], [[Material Plane]], the [[The Netherworld|Netherworld]] and [[The Void]]. All of which are in turn separated by the [[Ethereal Plane]].

#### Elemental Planes
The Elemental Plane refers to the planes of elemental air, wood, water, metal, earth, and fire located within the Inner Sphere of the Great Beyond. They are formed of raw fundamental matter which serves as the physical building blocks for the Material Plane and most demiplanes.

+ [[Plane of Air]]
+ [[Plane of Earth]]
+ [[Plane of Fire]]
+ [[Plane of Metal]]
+ [[Plane of Water]]
+ [[Plane of Wood]]

#### Transitive Planes
At a minimum, each Transitive Plane coexists with one or more other planes, a relationship oversimplified by stating that Transitive Planes are a way of getting from one plane to another. Each of these planes overlap in some way with nearly all Planes.

+ [[Astral Plane]]
+ [[Ethereal Plane]]
+ [[First World]]
+ [[The Netherworld]]

#### Energy Planes
The Energy Planes refer to two planes that generate polar opposite forces. These two forces balance each other, and work in perfect harmony.

+ [[Creation's Forge]], the source of all life.
+ [[The Void]], the final end.

## The Outer Sphere
While the Inner Sphere contains the elemental planes, the Outer Sphere contains nine planes, each strongly aligned to a particular philosophy. Each Outer Plane is a stable realm within the chaotic [[The Maelstrom|Maelstrom]].

These Planes are the dwellings of [[Gods|Deities]], as well as the destination of the [[Lifestream]], and the afterlife for the departed souls of mortals.

#### Neutral Planes
These planes are associated with neutrality.

+ [[Axis]], the city of law and order.
+ [[The Boneyard]], the lands of judgement.
+ [[The Maelstrom]], the chaotic space between.

#### Lower Planes
These planes, also called the Unholy Planes, are associated with evil, unholiness and fiends.

+ [[Abaddon]], the apocalyptic lands of Daemons.
+ [[Hell]], the nine-layered pit of Devils.
+ [[The Abyss]], the infinite depths of Demons.

#### The Upper Planes
These plans, also called the Celestial planes, are associated with good, holiness and celestials.

+ [[Elysium]], the golden realm.
+ [[Heaven]], the great paradise.
+ [[Nirvana]], the sanctuary of enlightenment.
