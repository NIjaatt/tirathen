The [[Material Plane]] is the prosaic realm and the home of mortal life. Innumerable galaxies play host to countless stars and their planets, each housing unique peoples and cultures. One of said planets, is [[Tirathen]]. The [[Material Plane]] serves as the destination for pre-incarnate souls, each mortal life born, living, and dying before sending its spirit toward the planes of the Outer Sphere for judgment. The [[Material Plane]] is likewise the focus of the gods, each of whom is invested in fostering their own particular portfolio among mortal worshippers and the material world at large.
