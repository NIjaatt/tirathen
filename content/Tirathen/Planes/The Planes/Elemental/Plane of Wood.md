The Elemental Plane of Wood, also known as the **Mother Forest**, is located in the Inner Sphere, nestled between the [[Plane of Air]] and the [[Plane of Water]]. It is a realm of ancient splendor and growth, eternally tended and shaped by the plane's inhabitants. The Mother Forest has no single ruler, but is instead presided over by a two elemental lords of wood, Yssavel and Eldros.

### Geography
The Plane of Wood is a plane made up entirely of a vibrant, and dense forest, filled with plant life. It's fractal tree growth stretches across the plane with dizzying, yet beautiful symmetry.

### Inhabitants
Creatures either enforce and augment the ordered growth of their home or exist as a byproduct thereof. Some drop from the trees as discarded seeds given fresh life, while others chisel or cultivate creatures’ natural beauty to suit their aesthetic tastes. The genies of the plane, kizidhars, are artisans in this field.

The elemental lords of wood embody the often-paradoxical rigidity and pliancy of their element. Eons ago, when the other planes began to war with each other, the lords of wood acted swiftly to seal off their home from potential invaders, thereby protecting the plane and its residents—yet even in this, they’re said to have disagreed, and the two haven’t spoken in millennia. Yssavel, the Carved Lady of Mimicry, desperately searches for a cure for her great ailment, while Eldros, Custodian of Oak and Ash, worries that ending the plane’s long isolation will lead to its ruin.
