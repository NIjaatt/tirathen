The Elemental Plane of Fire, also known as the **Eternal Furnace**, is the outermost of the elemental planes, encircling the [[Plane of Earth]] to one side, and bordering the [[Astral Plane]] to the other. It is a realm of vast, liquid oceans of flame, charred cinderlands, and flowing rivers of magma. The Eternal Furnace is ruled by [[Soloun]], the Inferno Queen.

### Geography
Of all the elemental planes, the Plane of Fire is generally the most hostile to mortal life. It is a landscape of white hot heat, with rivers and seas of fire, lava and molten metal, mountains of compacted ash, and burning skies filled with smoke and curtains of flame.

### Inhabitants
Counter-intuitively, the Plane of Fire has the most varied forms of life of any of the elemental planes. It is home not only to countless fire elementals, but also to the ifrits, a cruel race of genies. They war with the kingdoms of the fire scamps and their enslaved azer.

Numerous other creatures call the Eternal Furnace home. Fire and magma elementals are abound throughout the plane, as are small magmin enclaves. Salamanders, magma dragons and rasts all have presences in the Plane of Fire as well. The enormous, wormlike thoqquas can be found along the borders of the Plane of Fire with the Plane of Earth, as can amorphous magma oozes. Fire whales originated on this plane, and pods of their kind can still be found swimming through the plane's fires.
