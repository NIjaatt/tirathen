The Elemental Plane of Metal, also known as the **Expanding Decay**, is located in the Inner Sphere, nestled between the [[Plane of Earth]] and the [[Plane of Water]]. It is a dissonant plane of chaotic decline. The Expanding Decay has no single ruler, but is instead presided over by a two elemental lords of metal, Ferradon and Thrammeil.

### Geography
The Plane of Metal is vast in scope and always expanding, just as much as it rusts and decays. Metal of every kind (liquid and solid), decaying cityscapes, and intense magnetic fields make up the chaotic structure of the plane.

### Inhabitants
Creatures reflect the jumble of forces that make their home. Many have fluid forms of flowing metal or inhabit clouds of rust. The genies of the plane, zuhras, put on bold performances keyed into the plane’s dissonance.

The elemental lords of metal embody the slow disintegration of their home. Many eons ago, the Plane of Earth assaulted the Plane of Metal, and the Metal Lords lost much of their realm and domain to the Fossilized King, [[Utharun]]. This led the lords into a period of great depression from which they are still struggling to overcome. Ferradon, the Sovereign of Alchemy, remains in deep slumber, while Thrammiel, the Lady of Rust, treads under the weight of deep despair.
