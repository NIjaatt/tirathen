Creation's Forge, also called the **Positive Energy Plane**, **Barúm's Furnace**, the **Cosmic Fire**, or **The Fires of Creation**—is the source of all life and vitality, and the birthplace of all of the souls in the [[The Great Beyond|Great Beyond]] and the source of the [[Lifestream]].

### Geography
Creation's Forge is a place of such overwhelming, fecund energy that all non-natives (including some deities) are generally incinerated within seconds of arrival unless appropriate precautions are taken.

The most apt comparison for the plane’s interior is that of the heart of a burning star. For indeed, the stars of the [[Material Plane]] each house natural portals to Creation’s Forge within their glowing, potent cores to foster the movement of pre-incarnate souls in their first steps in the great cycle of life and death.

### Inhabitants
Brilliant and blinding, the plane’s interior is sparsely populated, and the resident phoenix-kin jyotis are intensely xenophobic. Dwelling in glimmering, radiant crystalline cities such as Arudrellisiir, they view themselves as gardeners and guardians of souls spawned from their realm’s burning quintessence. Intensely distrustful of gods and their servitors, jyotis can nonetheless be bargained with, and they’ve frequently taken into their custodianship any number of artifacts and imprisoned beings considered too dangerous to house on any other plane.
