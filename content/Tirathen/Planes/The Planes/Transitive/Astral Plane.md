The Astral Plane, also known as the **Silver Sea**, surrounds the planes of the Inner Sphere, separating them from those of the Outer Sphere. The Astral Plane provides the backdrop against which the [[Lifestream]] flows from the [[Material Plane]], ushering departed spirits toward [[The Boneyard]] for final judgment.

### Geography
Far from an empty void, the Astral's silver substance churns with currents and storms from the metaphysical heat of the[[ Plane of Fire]], and where it touches the chaos of the [[The Maelstrom|Maelstrom]], the resulting eddies interact with the memories of the dead to produce fleeting simulacra and even demiplanes.

### The Lifestream
The only true constant within the Astral Plane are the countless branches of the Lifestream: the never-ending river of souls making their way from the Material Plane to their final destination in the Outer Sphere. Carried along by a silvery haze, most inhabitants of the Astral Plane give the Lifestream a wide berth, or else seem incapable of perceiving it; it is unknown whether this is because of a law of the Multiverse or by deific fiat. The Lifestream is monitored (either because of self-interest or benevolence) by countless servants of the gods, who help protect the souls from predators, and help to ensure that they finally reach the [[the Boneyard|Boneyard]].

### Inhabitants
The Silver sea is a place emptiness, and is made up of travellers only. Rare it is to find a true natural inhabitant of the Astral Plane. Travellers within the Astral find the plane untouched by the passage of time, a property exploited by many mortals fearing old age. Time, however, isn’t easily escaped, and upon exiting the Astral Plane, a creature finds this debt catching up to them, potentially aging to dust in moments.
