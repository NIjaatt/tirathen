The River Styx is an interplanar river that originates in the plane of [[Abaddon]], but snakes its way throughout the Outer Sphere and passing through the planes of [[Hell]], [[the Abyss]], and [[the Maelstrom]], before finally pooling at the base of [[Nephira]]'s Spire in [[Axis]].

### In Abaddon
On the plane of Abaddon, the Styx is the undisputed realm of Charon, Rider of Death. It is also home to the vicious hydrodaemons, who lurk beneath its surface, ready to attack anyone who survives the deleterious effects of the river. The surface is patrolled by thanadaemons, who pilot their skiffs and have even been known to ferry travelers across the Styx.

The Styx also feeds the Plaguemere, the colossal swamps where Apollyon, Rider of Pestilence, resides.

### In Hell
As it winds its way through Hell, the Styx feeds the swamps of Stygia, the plane's fifth layer.

### In the Abyss
The Styx passes through or is connected to a number of Abyssal Realms.
