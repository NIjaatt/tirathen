The Lifestream is the name given to the constant metaphysical migration of mortal souls from [[Creation's Forge]], to the [[Material Plane]], and lastly to [[Nephira]]'s [[The Boneyard|Boneyard]] in the Outer Sphere, where they are judged and sent to their eternal fate.

Cyclical in nature, upon a mortal being's death the Lifestream represents a soul's afterlife, and before birth the quintessence that becomes a new soul traverses the Lifestream and arrives in the Material Plane to give life to a new mortal being.

### Appearance
The only place the Lifestream is even potentially visible, is on the [[Astral Plane]]. Where the Lifestream appears from a distance like a massive, winding silver thread. Those who approach can see the countless souls moving through a silver haze, like so many schools of fish.

### Cycle of a Soul
The designed path of a mortal soul after death, in gross summary and with countless exceptions in practice, is as follows, with an indeterminate amount of time, moments in some, ages in others, passing between steps:

1. A soul's journey begins at [[Creation's Forge]], where a blank, empty soul enters the Lifestream.
2. As it passes through the [[First World]], the chaotic energies of the plane attach themselves alongside the positive life essence of the soul.
3. The soul then arrives on the [[Material Plane]], where the soul settles and becomes a single living being.
4. This 'settled' state of a soul remains, but the pull of the Lifestream is strong, forcing the soul's body to age.
5. Which, at the end of a mortal's life, the soul re-enters the Lifestream via the [[Ethereal Plane]], and travels along towards Nephira's [[The Boneyard|Boneyard]].
6. At the Boneyard the soul is judged and sorted into the appropriate afterlife. However the soul is allowed to dissent and potentially choose their own afterlife if they so wish.
7. Judged souls proceed to their assigned planes, becoming petitioners. 
8. Petitioners, too, die in time, becoming absorbed by and expanding their associated plane, as their own spiritual essence joins with the plane's as a substance known as quintessence.
9. The ever-churning [[The Maelstrom|Maelstrom]] breaks these planes down, scattering this planar quintessence throughout the Maelstrom and adjoining planes.
10. Those pieces that land on the Creation's Forge eventually form new blank souls and the cycle begins anew.
