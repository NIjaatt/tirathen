## Common Ancestries
Ancestries that are common are generally known and commonly witnessed in everyday life.

#### [[Beastfolk]]
Beastfolk refer to many different kinds and subspecies of sentient, bipedal animal people.

#### [[Dwarf]]
Dwarves are a short, stocky people who are often stubborn, fierce, and devoted.

#### [[Gnome]]
Gnomes are a short and hardy folk, with an unquenchable curiosity and eccentric habits.

#### [[Goblin]]
Goblins are a short, scrappy, energetic people who have spent millennia maligned and feared.

#### [[Halfling]]
Halflings are a short, resilient people who exhibit remarkable curiosity and humour.

#### [[Human]]
Humans are diverse, adaptable and ambitious people that arrived on Tirathen from another world.

#### [[Kobold]]
Kobolds are small and reptilian, with features marked by the power they choose to follow.

#### [[Orc]]
Orcs are proud, strong people with hardened physiques who value physical might and glory in combat.

## Uncommon Ancestries
Ancestries that are uncommon are generally known and but uncommonly witnessed in everyday life.

#### [[Athamaru]]
Athamarus are fish-like humanoids who form tight-knit communities led by a matriarch.

#### [[Centaur]]
Centaurs are half-human, half-horse nomads who range wild and free.

#### [[Hobgoblin]]
Hobgoblins are taller and stronger than their goblin cousins, often arranging themselves into military structures.

#### [[Leshy]]
Leshies are immortal nature spirits placed in small plant bodies, seeking to experience the world.

#### [[Minotaur]]
Minotaurs are horned, bovine humanoids who originate from an ancient divine curse.

## Rare Ancestries
Ancestries that are rare are known by the knowledgeable and are rarely witnessed in everyday life.

#### [[Automaton]]
These intelligent constructs house actual souls and represent what remains of a dying empire's last attempt at greatness. Automatons combine technological ingenuity with magical power, creating a blended being wholly unique to Tirathen.

#### [[Elf]]
Elves are a tall, long-lived people with a strong tradition of art and magic. Rarely do Elves leave their isolated home of [[Lands of Valeniir|Valeniir]].

#### [[Surki]]
Surkis are an insectile and highly metamorphic species that were born from magic, and are constantly evolving.
