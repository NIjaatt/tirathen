_Beastfolk refer to many different kinds and subspecies of sentient, bipedal animal people._

A unique ancestry, that could rather be described as a racial group instead of a single ancestry. For all animal-like creatures with sentience are given the blanket term of "beastfolk".

In the many thousands of years since the initial conception of beastfolk by grace of [[Orilyn]], these creatures have formed their own societies. Of all the animals, six kinds in particular rose above all their animal brethren to become the natural leaders of all beastfolk. Those being, the Catfolk, the Kholo, the Lizardfolk, the Ratfolk, the Tengu, and the Tripkee. All other beastfolk are colloquially referred to as Awakened Animals, but the Six are often revered and greatly respected by each other, and all other beastfolk.

### [[Awakened Animal]]
_Awakened animals colloquially refer to all animals not a part of the six main beastfolk ancestries. Awakened animals have one paw in nature and the other in the world of cities and society._

### [[Catfolk]]
_Catfolk are highly social, feline humanoids prone to curiosity and wandering._

### [[Kholo]]
_Kholos are hyena-headed humanoids who embrace practicality and pragmatism._

### [[Lizardfolk]]
_Lizardfolk are scaled humanoids with a history that stretches into the distant past._

### [[Ratfolk]]
_Ratfolk are small, clever, and adaptable humanoids with ratlike features and a love of community._

### [[Tengu]]
_Tengus are gregarious and resourceful avian humanoids who collect knowledge and treasures alike._

### [[Tripkee]]
_Tripkees are froglike humanoids whose spirit often exceeds their stature._
