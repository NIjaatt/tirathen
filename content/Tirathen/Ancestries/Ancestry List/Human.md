_Humans are diverse and adaptable people with wide potential and deep ambitions._

As unpredictable and varied as any of [[Tirathen]]'s peoples, humans have exceptional drive and the capacity to endure and expand. Despite their comparatively short time on Tirathen compared to the native races, humans have built some of the greatest and the most terrible societies throughout the course of Tirathen's history, and today they are the most populous people in the realms.

Humans' ambition, versatility, and tenacity have led to their status as the world's predominant ancestry. Their empires and nations are vast, sprawling things, and their citizens carve names for themselves with the strength of their sword arms and the power of their spells. Humanity is diverse and tumultuous, running the gamut from nomadic to imperial, sinister to saintly. Many of them venture forth to explore, to search for long-lost treasure, or to lead mighty armies to conquer their neighbours. For no better reason than because they can.

### Humans Typically
- Strive to achieve greatness, either in their own right or on behalf of a cause.
- Seek to understand their purpose in the world.
- Cherish their relationships with family and friends.

### Other Ancestries Probably...
- Respect a Human's flexibility, adaptability, and in most cases, their open-mindedness.
- Distrust a Human's intentions, fearing they seek only power or wealth.
- Aren't sure what to expect from Humans and are hesitant to assume their intentions.
- Outright despise Humans for their connection to [[the Spellplague]].

### Physical Description
Humans' physical characteristics are as varied as the world's climes. Humans have a wide variety of skin and hair colours, body types, and facial features. Generally speaking, their skin has a darker hue the closer to the equator they or their ancestors lived.

Masculine humans are on average taller and more broadly built, with heavier musculature and more angular facial features, and they more commonly grow facial hair. While feminine humans tend to be slightly shorter and more slender, with softer facial contours and wider hips.
  
Humans reach physical adulthood around the age of 15-16, though mental maturity occurs at 18-20. A typical human can live to be around 90 years old. Humans have exceptionally mutable physical characteristics compared to other ancestries, with greater variance in height, weight, and other physical parameters.

### Beliefs
Human beliefs are as varied as they are numbered. Views and beliefs are typically passed down from parent to child, but some seek their own path and cultivate their own views on the world.

### Society
Human variety also manifests in terms of their governments, attitudes, and social norms. Though the oldest of human cultures can trace their shared histories thousands of years into the past, when compared to the societies of the [[Elf|Elves]] or [[Dwarf|Dwarves]], human civilizations seem in a state of constant flux as empires fragment and new kingdoms subsume the old.

From societal expectations, to religious practices, no human society is the same. The fashion trends and speech patterns of [[Korvia]] will not be the same as [[Valdyr|Valdyr's]].

#### Language
All Humans speak Trade Common, their native language.

#### Religion
Just as their societies and beliefs differ, humans worship whichever god they want. Though they hold strong faith in whichever god they choose to worship, likely not abandoning their faith for their entire lives. That being said, there are some humans that choose to worship no god, and carve their own paths unbeholden to the divine.
