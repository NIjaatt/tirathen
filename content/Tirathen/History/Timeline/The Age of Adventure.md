The Age of Adventure is the present age of [[Tirathen]]. Where adventurers seek glory and treasures left behind by ancient civilisations.

This time is ripe with political strife, as countries seek to regain lost lands or take more for themselves. But forces unseen stir, the world is changing, ancient evils awaking. It's in the air, the people feel it yet dare not utter it.
