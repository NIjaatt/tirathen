In a time of peace and tranquillity, everything changed. Marking the start of his Age was a portal from another world opening in the western heartlands of [[Korvia]], and creatures not native to [[Tirathen]] stepped foot on the land. Humans.

Humans brought with them Magic, which infected the land and it's people like a plague. Killing a significant number of the native peoples of Tirathen in an event known as [[The Spellplague]].
