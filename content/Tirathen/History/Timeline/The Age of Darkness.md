In the twilight years of [[The Age of Suffering]], the natives peoples were finally beginning to recover from the significant loss of life caused by [[The Spellplague]].

That was so, until in 1808 P.D, when an asteroid crashed into the moon of [[Belegyr]]. Sending fragments of the partially destroyed moon raining down upon the lands, and unleashing the spawn of [[Nhalgorath]] upon its denizens. The fragments of the partially destroyed moon settled in the atmosphere, blotting out the sun for 500 years.
