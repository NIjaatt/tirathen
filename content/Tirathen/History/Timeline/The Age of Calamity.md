During this age, many terrors were unleashed upon the world. Terrors that are still used in legend and folktales to the present day.

For what marked the beginning of this age, was the birth of [[The Dooms of Tirathen]]. Titanic monster that were each a force of nature, wiping out the peoples of [[Tirathen]] as they rampaged across the lands. Leaving the people terrified for nearly a century.

However, in the twilight of the century, heroes rose and fought against the Dooms of Tirathen. Pushing them back, and even slaying one. Many of these heroes ascended to divinity, and make up the [[Heroic Pantheon]].
