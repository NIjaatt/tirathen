With [[The Dooms of Tirathen]] defeated, the denizens of [[Tirathen]] could begin to rebuild. Life flourishes once more, no longer under threat. More civilisations are formed, and thrive.

However, the world is not without strife. In the wake of the [[Heroic Pantheon|Heroic Pantheon's]] rise, many heroes and villains known to history make their appearance in this age.

Some of these legends include;
Tyr Al'Bahir before his resurrection as the Lich King.

Caedric Azaed, the First Dragon Slayer.

Drustan the Tall who held off an advancing army alone.

Corvin of the Winds, a tactical mastermind who changed the way war was waged forever.

Ioldar, the Sculptor who created the Elf Gates, and their power source, the Sovyrian Stone. 

There were many more heroes and villains, but these are the most well known mortals to the current day.
