The Great Collapse was a cataclysmic event that took place in 1508 P.D. A large asteroid crashed into the moon of [[Belegyr]]. Sending fragments of the partially destroyed moon down into [[Tirathen]]. Raining the lands in fire and destruction, and unleashing the spawn of [[Nhalgorath]] upon the lands.

## Consequences
It resulted in the destruction of towns and cities worldwide. Earthquakes, and massive tidal waves wreaked over the lands, ending many lives that were already suffering at the hands of [[The Spellplague]] that was ongoing at the same time.

The cloud of dust thrown up by the shattered moon's fragmented impact settled in Tirathen's upper atmosphere and blotted out the sun for 500 years, plunging the world into an [[The Age of Darkness|Age of Darkness]].

### The Great Reset
Before the Great Collapse, many civilisations and peoples were beginning to harmonise with the presence of magic, and were achieving technological and magical breakthroughs unequalled even in the present day. The Great Collapse brought all of this to an end, in effect resetting these developments back hundreds of years.

### Formation of the Sunken Sea
The largest fragment of [[Belegyr]] landed in the northern lands of [[Valdyr]], forming a massive crater which separated the two norths that were once previously connected. The crater filled with water from the Boreal Ocean, forming the Sunken Sea.

### Flight of the Elves
Having been forewarned of the coming disaster by their oracles, and having suffered severely at the hands of the Human Invasion some few hundred years earlier. Most of the Elves of [[Tirathen]] fled to their native lands of [[Lands of Valeniir|Valeniir]] and have remained there in seclusion to the present day.
