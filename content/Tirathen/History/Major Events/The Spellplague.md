In a time of peace and tranquillity, everything changed. In 1202 P.D a portal from another world opened in the western heartlands of [[Korvia]], and creatures not native to [[Tirathen]] stepped foot on the land.

### The Arrival of Humans & Magic
The most prominent of these creatures, were [[Human|Humans]]. At first, relations between the off-worlders and the native peoples of Tirathen were peaceful. Humans were given permission to house a few settlements on the Elvish lands of [[Korvia]]. However, something else entered Tirathen by way of this portal. Magic; a mysterious supernatural force that almost seems to be just as alive as any other, spread through the lands of Tirathen, granting its boons to many. However, Magic is not native to the lands or peoples of Tirathen, their bodies not acclimated to hold such power.

What was once a boon, quickly become poison as the magic killed its hosts. It continued to spread through the lands, infecting the natives of Tirathen like a plague and killing them. It is estimated that roughly 80% of all native life was wiped out by what was dubbed, the Spellplague. Only thanks to the intervention of the Eight Makers, who dared not see their creations perish, were the native folk of Tirathen spared from extinction.

### The Human Invasion
As the natives of Tirathen were being wiped out by the Spellplague, the [[Human|Humans]] saw chance to capitalize on the weakness of their new home. Their bodies already acclimated to magic, they used it in force to conquer, taking the lands of [[Korvia]] and [[Valdyr]] with ease. The [[Halmyr|Holy Kingdom of Halmyr]] responded, launching the Second [[The Holy Crusades|Holy Crusade]] in their history against the invaders of Tirathen. However, the campaign ultimately failed as their armies fell to the Spellplague, and despite having a mighty ground force, there was naught Halmyr could do in the face of the magic wielding humans.

### Lasting Effect on Tirathen
Many followers of the [[Old Pantheon]] believe the Spellplague is a punishment upon the world for the felling of Valloryn's [[World Tree]]'s.
