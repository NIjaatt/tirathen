Thousands of years ago now, when the Dooms of Tirathen plagued the lands. Each one a force of nature, capable of wiping out civilisations, rampaged across the lands. Leaving the people of Tirathen scared and afraid for nearly a century. Within the twilight of the century, Heroes rose, and fought back [[the Dooms of Tirathen]], slaying one and pushing back the rest. Many of these heroes become [[Gods|Deities]], rising to join the ranks of the [[Heroic Pantheon]].  These heroes have not been lost to time, and are still worshipped in the current era.
