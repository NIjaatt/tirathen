For many thousands of years, the [[Beastfolk]] have been treated with distain and have been subject to racism and slavery for their biological similarities to animals.

In more recent years, the Beastfolk as an race began to become aware of just how poorly they were treated on [[Valloryn]] compared to their home continent of [[Telmaria]]. This resulted in a series of slave uprisings across the continent of Valloryn, which culminated in the deaths of many over the course of 30 years.

By 1250 A.C, the majority of the other races of Valloryn had become sympathetic to the Beastfolk, which allowed a path for their status to be elevated. By 1259 A.C nearly all nations of Valloryn had recognised the Beastfolk as a sentient race and people.

However, the [[Halmyr|Holy Kingdom of Halmyr]] still views the Beastfolk as lesser, "dirty" beings. A vast number of Beastfolk slaves are still used in Halmyr in the present day.
