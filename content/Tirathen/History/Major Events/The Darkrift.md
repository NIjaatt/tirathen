In 937 P.D, The Holy Kingdom of Halmyr had launched a campaign to purge a cult dedicated to a demon lord. By 940 P.D, they had rooted the heretics out, and successfully defending the Holy Kingdom from demonic influence.

However, the cult was not finished as they reorganized themselves in the [[Taldar|Kingdom of Taldar]], just to the north of Halmyr. Some five years later, a witch, working in conjunction with her demonic patron, opened The Darkrift. Demons poured out, and overran the nation of Taldar. As the rift grew everlarger, it came to be seen as a threat to the entire world. Thus began the [[The Holy Crusades|First Crusade]].

The First Crusade ended in 968 P.D, with the sealing of Taldar by the [[Gates of Ikanos]]. Great wardstones, imbued with divine power were placed along the borders of Taldar and kept the demonic forces from passing.

The Darkrift still exists within Taldar in the present day, the lands still overrun by demon kind.
