The Obellian Empire is believed to be one of the first nations to establish itself on [[Valloryn]] early on in [[The Age of Founding|the Age of Founding]]. Unknown, ruined yet technologically advanced structures span almost the entirety of Valloryn, yet their relation to each other and the Obellian Empire was not connected until 1293 A.C.

### Lands
The Obellian Empire is believed to have ruled over the majority of Valloryn from the early years of [[The Age of Founding|the Age of Founding]] and ruled for over a millennium before falling roughly around the time of [[The Age of Calamity]]. 

At is height, it dominated all of modern day, Korvia, the Frozen North, Valdyr and Elovan, as well as Taldar. This is evidenced all across these lands, where some of the deepest and oldest ruins are clearly of Obellian design.

### Culture
Little is known of these truly ancient people, as time has erased most remnants of this once great culture, although it is assumed that they were of Elvish ethnicity, likely of a heritage lost to evolution. It is certain however, given the time period that these people were incredibly gifted mechanically. A skill they used to likely dominate and conquer their other, less advanced neighbours until they controlled the majority of Valloryn. 

### Automatons
In recent years, a number of mechanical creations found in their ruins have began reawakening. These beings, dubbed [[Automaton|Automatons]], are few and a rare sight indeed. Most were captured to be studied and tested, yet some wander the world seeking their purpose, having lost their original purpose to time and rust.
