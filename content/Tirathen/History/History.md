## Timeline
### The Age of Creation ??? ~ 1001 P.C
Pre-history. There are many accounts and ideas of what happened in this time. Only one thing is known for sure about this time, and that is the Gods created Tirathen and its denizens.

### [[The Age of Founding]] 1000 ~ 1 P.C
Life begins to truly flourish, the first civilisations are founded. It is believed that the [[Obellia|Obellian Empire]] was first founded and existed around this time.

### [[The Age of Calamity]] 1 ~ 100 P.D
[[The Dooms of Tirathen]], titanic monsters, are born. Each one a force of nature, wiping out the peoples of Tirathen as they rampaged across the lands. Leaving the people scared and afraid for nearly a century. Within the twilight of the century, Heroes rose, and fought back the Dooms of Tirathen, killing some and pushing back the rest. Many of these heroes become [[Gods|Deities]].

### [[The Age of Legend]] 101 ~ 1201 P.D
Mortal life begins to flourish once more, no longer under threat of the Dooms of Tirathen. Civilisations thrive. Yet the world is not without strife. In the wake of the [[Heroic Pantheon|Heroic Pantheon's]] rise, many heroes and villains known to history make their appearance in this age.

### [[The Age of Suffering]] 1202 ~ 1808 P.D
Humans arrive on Tirathen from another world. Bringing with them magic, which infects the land and its people like a plague. Killing 80% of the native peoples of Tirathen. 

### [[The Age of Darkness]] 1808 P.D ~ 499 A.C
An asteroid crashed into the moon of [[Belegyr]]. Sending fragments of the partially destroyed moon raining down upon the lands, and unleashing the spawn of [[Nhalgorath]] upon its denizens. The fragments of the partially destroyed moon settled in the atmosphere, blotting out the sun for 500 years.

### [[The Age of Adventure]] 500 ~ 1345 A.C
The present age of Tirathen. Where adventurers seek glory and treasures left behind by ancient civilisations. This time is ripe with political strife, as countries seek to regain lost lands or take more for themselves. But forces unseen stir, the world is changing, ancient evils awaking. It's in the air, the people feel it yet dare not utter it.
