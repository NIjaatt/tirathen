As mortal life began on Tirathen, something took notice. An ancient, unknowable creature from beyond [[The Great Beyond]], ripped and burrowed its way into the [[Material Plane]] made by the [[Gods|Deities]]. It came with only one goal in mind. To destroy and consume. [[Nhalgorath]], the Destroyer consumed countless worlds within the [[Material Plane]] and relished in the taste, yet it only sought to consume more.

The [[Gods|Deities]], still bickering and fighting amongst each other were slow to notice [[Nhalgorath]]'s presence. That was until the Destroyer turned its gaze upon [[Tirathen]]. [[Radia]], the Great Mother was the first to notice the presence of [[Nhalgorath]] and rallied together her siblings. The Celestial & Dark Pantheon joining in unison once more to defend their creations from the great enemy.

Many [[Gods|Deities]], now forgotten to time died in the battle; certainly the deities that sided with the Destroyer will never be remembered. Their numerous clashes against [[Nhalgorath]] became known as the Dawn War. The [[Gods|Deities]] were victorious numerous times, yet the Destroyer always recovered more powerful than before. So they decided to seal Nhalgorath.

[[Seralyn]] lured  [[Nhalgorath]], away from Tirathen into a trap where many [[Gods|Deities]] waited. As they fought, [[Barúm]] began constructing a second Moon for Tirathen, one that would act as a prison for the Destroyer, imbued with potent divine wards against escape. [[Aeyora]] then sliced open a rift into the moon and hurled [[Nhalgorath]] into it's prison. [[Baelmir]] impaled him with spears of pure stars forged by [[Felurion]] that prevented [[Nhalgorath]] from hearing prayers and receiving worship while [[Asmodeus]] bound him within the moon with a key crafted by [[Oranthys]] that only [[Asmodeus]] could turn.

[[Aeyora]] closed the rift; leaving behind a smooth scar upon the moon of [[Belegyr]]. [[Nhalgorath|Nhalgorath's]] defeat marked the end of the Dawn War, and the end of the Age of Creation.
