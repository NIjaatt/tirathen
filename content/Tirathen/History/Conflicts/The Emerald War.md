For ages past, The Emerald Sea and the surrounding oceans had been locked in a dispute of ownership by many pirate lords. In the current [[The Age of Adventure|Age of Adventure]], such disputes ended as four of the most prominent pirate lords of the current era joined hands in the creation of the [[Fourfold Dominion]], a nation of pirates in 1335 A.C. These pirate lords were Lohtala Searunner, Qirik the Crow, Sarl of the Wind, and Amir Al'Bahir. However, issues arouse when each pirate lord wished to rule their newly founded nation by themselves. So the idea came to light to hold one last naval battle between the four lords.

### The Battle of Four
The battle between the four pirate lords begun in 1335 A.C and went on for a year. A number of large scale skirmishes took place all across the Emerald Sea. This battle was the culmination of years of conflict and strife between the Four Lords each vying for dominance, and the coastal assaults they embarked upon after unifying under a sole banner. In 1336 A.C, the battle was ended. The sole victor, and the unanimous leader of the Fourfold Dominion was from then on Amir Al'Bahir, a Human that bore the reviled last name of the villainous Lich King, a branding of the traitors that joined hands with the Lich King some 400 years ago.

### The [[Rising of the Athamaru]]
As Amir rose to become the Pirate King, something else rose from the Emerald Sea in 1337 A.C. The [[Athamaru]], a race of sentient fish-like people once deemed a myth and folktale, rose from the ocean seeking refuge in the lands above. Fourfold Dominion fought back against the invading Athamaru, pushing them south onto the isle of [[Nyrrath]] where the Athamaru remain to the current day.
