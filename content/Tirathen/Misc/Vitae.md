Vitae are a broad classification of ephemeral creatures that are composed of and defined by their vital essence. They are sometimes erroneously referred to as "nature spirits", but are not considered to be spirits, which are creatures composed of and defined by spiritual essence.

The most well-known examples of vitae are the precursor energies that combine with a spiritual essence to animate a [[Leshy]].

Vitae are not composed of quintessence, meaning they are not mortal beings and are functionally immortal.
