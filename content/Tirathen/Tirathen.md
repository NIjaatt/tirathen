The world of Tirathen orbits a yellow sun somewhere in the vast expanse of the Universe. Third of twelve in orbit, this blue planet contains vast oceans, and lush green lands, the perfect environment for countless cultures to thrive. Indeed, Tirathen is the most populous planet in its solar system. Tirathen's two moons, [[Llunys]] and [[Belegyr]], fills its night sky, and its nearest planetary neighbours, Dalivan, the Green World and Kalitesh the Red Planet (Second and Fourth from the sun, respectively), are easily visible as coloured stars.

Due to its innately hospitable and life-sustaining environment compared to other worlds, presumably the work of the deities. Astronomers sometimes refer to Tirathen as the "First Miracle"

### Geography
Tirathen is believed to be roughly 40 million years old, at which primeval point its environment was hostile to modern forms of life, and its landscapes were dominated by prehistoric beasts. Dragons joined such beasts in the planet's prehistoric ages from that period to approximately 1 million years ago.

Life only truly began by grace of the gods at some point between 10,000 and 5,000 years ago, with recorded history only beginning 2,000 years ago.

### Continents
Tirathen currently contains two known continents amid an immense ocean and various seas.

[[Valloryn]], is Tirathen's largest continent, spanning much of the world's east, spanning from the far north, to the deep south.

[[Nel'Doreth]], a continent easily forgotten. The great tides of the Dividing Sea sometimes cover this land entirely, hiding the continent for decades at a time. It has recently resurfaced once again.

[[Telmaria]], is a lush continent west of Valloryn, separated by the Dividing Sea.

### Oceans
Four great oceans cover the remainder of Tirathen.

+ The **Boreal Ocean**, is the northernmost body of water known for it's immense chill.
+ The **Outer Ocean**, is the westernmost body of water which spans far and wide.
+ The **Abyssal Ocean**, is the easternmost body of water and is immeasurably deep.
+ The **Torrid Ocean**, is the southernmost body of water known for it's warmth.

### Seas
The four most prominent seas of Tirathen are:

+ The Emerald Sea, that surrounds the [[Emerald Isles]] on Valloryn.
+ The Dividing Sea, that separates Telmaria and Valloryn.
+ The Inner Sea, the body of water that separates Telmaria from Nel'Doreth.
+ The Sunken Sea, the once rich land sunken by a piece of the moon, [[Belegyr]] during [[the Great Collapse]].
