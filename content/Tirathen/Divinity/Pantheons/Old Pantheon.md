The Old Pantheon is the assembly of the Primordial Gods. These gods are representations of the four major elements. They are called the Old Pantheon because in the early days of Tirathen, these gods were the first to be worshipped before the discovery of the other Pantheons.

Gods of the Old Pantheon are considered to be the most interactive with the world of Tirathen due to their influence over their primary element.
