The Dark Pantheon is the assembly of the Darks Gods of Tirathen. These gods were formed alongside the gods of the [[Celestial Pantheon]], and helped form the world of Tirathen with them. However, members of the Dark Pantheon disliked how their brethren began to create individual [[Ancestry Overview|Ancestries]], and hated how they became attached to them.

Together they formed the Dark Pantheon, and created all kinds of monsters. Beings unnatural. Demons, Devils, twisted and malformed creatures and peoples. A mockery of their brethren's creations.

The Dark Pantheon is made up of two groups, being the Four Rulers, and the Eight Lords.

### The Four Rulers
The Four Rulers are the Dark Gods that rule over the remaining Eight Lords. Their strength unmatched.

- [[Asmodeus]], the Betrayer
- [[Dravathis]], the Demon Queen
- [[Kalazhar]], the Calamity Dragon
- [[Zarithus]], the Gossamer King

### The Eight Lords
The Eight Lords are the Dark Gods that serve the Four Rulers, whether by devotion or fear.

- [[Baelmir]], the Dark Prince
- [[Calara]], the Scion of Death
- [[Khaesor]], the Decaying End
- [[Morvyn]], the Scoundrel King
- [[Nyrrath]], the Lord of Blood
- [[Thessia]], the Vampire Queen
- [[Tomen]], the Dark Seer
- [[Ythiris]], the Lady of Despair
