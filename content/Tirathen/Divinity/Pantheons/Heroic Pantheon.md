The Heroic Pantheon is the assembly of the Great Heroes of Tirathen. These gods are worshipped and celebrated heroes of past ages who fought against great evils and triumphed. This worship and praise allowed for them to ascend upon the end of their mortals lives, becoming a true deity.

Gods of the Heroic Pantheon are much more involved with the material plane than the Celestial and Dark gods.

One god exists as a member of the Heroic Pantheon who doesn't quite fit however. This is because he is a god outside of divine logic. He doesn't embody aspects of a Celestial God, nor a Dark God. He has always existed, and will be the last thing to exist for he is the Harbinger of the End.
