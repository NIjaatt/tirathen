**Soloun** (pronounced Soul-en) is the primordial god of fire. She dwells on the [[Plane of Fire]]. She wages a never-ending war against the other denizens of the Plane of Fire, and against the Plane of Earth.

The Inferno Queen is a member of the [[Old Pantheon]] and holds domain over the element of Fire.

**Edicts:** Inspire with zeal and strategy, be passionate and quick of wit, destroy your foes with fire
**Anathema:** Allow yourself to stagnate or lose motivation, extinguish destructive blazes
**Areas of Concern:** Fire, heat, smoke
**Religious Symbol:** Four flaming swords
**Sacred Animal:** Salamander
**Sacred Colour(s):** Red
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Old Pantheon]]
