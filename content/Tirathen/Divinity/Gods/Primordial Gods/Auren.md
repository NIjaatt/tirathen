**Auren** (pronounced Oar-Ren) is the primordial god of air. He dwells on the [[Plane of Air]]. Auren is known for his erratic nature, he is both cruel and capricious, as mercurial as the wind itself.

The Emperor of Wind is a member of the [[Old Pantheon]] and holds domain over the element of Air.

**Edicts:** Revel in formlessness and freedom, humiliate terrestrial creatures, kill foes via falling or hazards from high winds
**Anathema:** Deny a flying creature the ability to [fly](https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2312), walk on earth if you could easily travel otherwise
**Areas of Concern:** Air, last breaths, windstorms
**Religious Symbol:** Wind Chimes.
**Sacred Animal:** Eagle
**Sacred Colour(s):** White
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Old Pantheon]]

**Favoured Weapon:** [longbow](https://2e.aonprd.com/Weapons.aspx?ID=436)
**Domains:** [air](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=65), [destruction](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=71), [fate](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=75), [travel](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=93)
**Cleric Spells:** 1st: [gust of wind](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1550), 2nd: [summon elemental](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1698) (water only), 4th: [vapor form](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1738)

### Divine Intercession
Auren is known to have never once granted a boon or curse to mortals.
