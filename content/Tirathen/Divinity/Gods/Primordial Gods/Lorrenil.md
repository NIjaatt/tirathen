**Lorrenil** (pronounced Lore-en-nill) is the primordial god of water. She dwells on the [[Plane of Water]]. It is said she spends much of her time slumbering, entertaining herself with rampages of wanton destruction and conquest whenever she wakes.

The Brine Empress is a member of the [[Old Pantheon]] and holds domain over the element of Water.

**Edicts:** Kill your foes by drowning them, sacrifice treasures to the depths of the ocean
**Anathema:** Destroy a body of water, use magic to calm the waves
**Areas of Concern:** Drowning, oceans, seas, water, waves
**Religious Symbol:** Notched shark's fin
**Sacred Animal:** Great White Shark
**Sacred Colour(s):** Blue
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Old Pantheon]]
