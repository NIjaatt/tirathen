**Utharun** (pronounced Uth-a-run) is the primordial god of Earth. He dwells on the [[Plane of Earth]]. Utharun is a relaxed and content god who is a mystery to even the denizens of his own domain. He concentrates his efforts solely against the Plane of Fire and his hated rival [[Soloun]], the Queen of the Inferno.

The Fossilized King is a member of the [[Old Pantheon]] and holds domain over the element of Earth.

**Edicts:** Obscure your true motives, slowly poison others, use the strength of stone to protect yourself and your secrets
**Anathema:** Make fire larger or hotter than necessary, remove a creature’s [petrified](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=87) condition
**Areas of Concern:** Bone, buried secrets, earth, radiation
**Religious Symbol:** Fossilized [dinosaur](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Dinosaur "Dinosaur") tooth
**Sacred Animal:** Giant Mole
**Sacred Colour(s):** Brown
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Old Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
Utharun in his disinterest of the material plane is known to have never once granted a boon or curse to mortals.
