**Khaesor** (pronounced Kay-Sore) is the goddess of unmaking and secrets. Her sole design seems to be the fragmenting of bonds, trust, and the destruction of the universe, though mediated by an obsession with planning and organization. Her three aspects as worshiped are mysteries, deception, and destruction.

The Decaying End is a member of the [[Dark Pantheon]], and is a close aid to [[Zarithus]] as one of the Eight Lords.

**Edicts:** Infiltrate righteous organizations and governments, destroy trust, perform mortal sacrifices
**Anathema:** Betray a fellow servant of Khaesor, harm those under Khaesor’s protection
**Areas of Concern:** Discord, eclipses, secrets
**Religious Symbol:** Six arms forming a hexagon
**Sacred Animal:** Owl
**Sacred Colour(s):** Black, orange
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Dark Pantheon]]
