**Dravathis** (pronounced Drav-A-Thiss) is the mother and patroness of many misshapen and malformed creatures, as well as monsters that crawl, slither, or flap on, above, or below the surface of [[Tirathen]]. Her religious symbol is a three-eyed jackal head, which may be represented in many ways. Dravathis revels in corruption of the pure. The physical changes and nightmares left by her interventions are treated as gifts by her followers and unwelcome terrors by the outside world. There are some that find respite or even a family among Dravathis's followers due to the faith’s greater acceptance of differences.

The Demon Queen is a member of the [[Dark Pantheon]], and is one of the Four Rulers that presides over the Pantheon.

**Edicts:** bring power to outcasts and the downtrodden, indoctrinate others in Dravathis’s teachings, make the beautiful monstrous, reveal the corruption and flaws in all things
**Anathema:** attempt to change that which makes you different, provide succor to Dravathis’s enemies
**Areas of Concern:** aberrance, monsters, and nightmares
**Religious Symbol:** three-eyed jackal
**Sacred Animal:** jackal
**Sacred Colour(s):** red, yellow
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Dark Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
Dravathis rewards kinship with monsters and brutal dominance in combat. Those who offend her serve the brood as prey or unwilling sacrifices.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Dravathis’s touch mutates a part of your body. You gain either an [unarmed](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=719) Strike that deals 1d6 damage or one that deals 1d4 damage and has the [finesse](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=602) and [agile](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=526) traits. Whether the attack deals bludgeoning, slashing, or piercing damage depends on the mutation. If the unarmed Strike replaces a limb, you can still use the mutated limb for its original functions. Dravathis chooses the form and function of your mutation.

**Moderate Boon**: You spread Dravathis’s nightmares everywhere you go. You can cast [_confusion_](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1471) once per day as a divine innate spell.

**Major Boon**: Dravathis uses your body to birth a new monster, regardless of your gender. Once per day, you can spend 1 minute to birth a monster determined by the GM, which rips its way from your belly. The monster’s level is up to your level, and it does as it pleases, following Dravathis’s will, though it doesn’t attack you unless you’ve lost her favor. You’re [drained](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=68) 3 from the ordeal.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Horrid visions torment your mind, overlaying reality at inopportune times. You treat everything around you as if it was [concealed](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=2419).

**Moderate Curse**: Dravathis marks you as prey. Any creature with imprecise or better [scent](https://2e.aonprd.com/MonsterAbilities.aspx?ID=74) can smell you from 100 × the usual range of their scent and can’t shake the feeling that you smell like prey, so they might attack you even if they would normally avoid attacking creatures of your ancestry. This smell doesn’t magically compel their action, and the scent of prey doesn’t overcome a deeper bond such as that with an animal companion.

**Major Curse**: Your dreams are an unending stream of nightmares. You need 16 hours of rest to try to get enough sleep to recover resources in daily preparation that normally require an 8-hour rest; even then, you must succeed at a DC 15 flat check to do so, and you’re still fatigued on a successful check.
