**Kalazhar** (pronounced Kal-A-Z-Arr) is the god of evil dragons, particularly the chromatic dragons. He is a god of destruction, chaos, rage, and draconic greed, the polar opposite of his father, [[Sarithar]] the Waybringer. However, he is a patient god capable of civility when the situation calls for it, as he bides his time. Waiting for the right moment to finally slay his father.

The Calamity Dragon is a member of the [[Dark Pantheon]], and is one of the Four Rulers that presides over the Pantheon.

- **Edicts:** kill dragons, destroy things at your whim, study past disasters
- **Anathema:** spare a foe after you have chosen to kill them, forgive a slight, prevent the destruction of things that cannot be saved
- **Areas of Concern:** destruction, greed, wicked dragons
- **Religious Symbol:** falling, burning scale
- **Sacred Animal:** none
- **Sacred Colour(s):** black, red, white
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Dark Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
Kalazhar is known to have never once granted a boon or curse to mortals.
