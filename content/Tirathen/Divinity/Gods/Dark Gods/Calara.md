**Calara** (pronounced Cal-Ar-Ah) is the goddess of runes, fate, and death. She was depicted either as a stern woman with no mouth, or as a creature with a snake's lower half, a human woman's upper half, six wings, and a Sihedron for a head.

The Scion of Death is a member of the [[Dark Pantheon]], and is a close aid to [[Kalazhar]] as one of the Eight Lords.

- **Edicts:** Work hard and demand others do so as well
- **Anathema:** Disobey a superior, destroy a book, shirk your duties
- **Areas of Concern:** Obedience, death, fate, runes
- **Religious Symbol:** Sihedron
- **Sacred Animal:** Snake
- **Sacred Colour(s):** Gold and green
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Dark Pantheon]]

### Devotee Benefits
- **Divine Attribute:** Intelligence or Charisma
- **Divine Font:** [harm](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=146) or [heal](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=148)
- **Divine Sanctification:** can choose holy or unholy
- **Divine Skill:** [Arcana](https://2e.aonprd.com/Skills.aspx?ID=35)
- **Favoured Weapon:** [whip](https://2e.aonprd.com/Weapons.aspx?ID=404)
- **Domains:** [fate](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=75), [trickery](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=94), [magic](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=82), [undeath](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=97)
- **Cleric Spells:** 1st: [mindlink](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1603), 2nd: [animus mine](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=2342), 6th: [never mind](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1614)

### Divine Intercession
Calara is known to have never once granted a boon or curse to mortals.
