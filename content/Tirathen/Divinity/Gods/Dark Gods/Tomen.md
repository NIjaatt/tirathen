**Tomen** (pronounced Tome-n) is the demon god of forbidden knowledge and magic. He is said to know countless magical formulae, spells, and secrets, including the Final Incantation, a single word so powerful its speaking causes the annihilation of magic. He specializes in magic and lore associated with pain and devastation.

The Dark Seer is a member of the [[Dark Pantheon]], and is a close aid to [[Kalazhar]] as one of the Eight Lords.

**Edicts:** Learn and hoard forbidden magic, steal secrets from others
**Anathema:** Destroy forbidden knowledge, reveal the entirety of a secret
**Areas of Concern:** Forbidden knowledge, magic, snakes
**Religious Symbol:** Demonic face and snakes
**Sacred Animal:** Snake
**Sacred Colour(s):** Green, orange
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Dark Pantheon]]
