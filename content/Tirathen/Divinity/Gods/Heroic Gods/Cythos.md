**Cythos** (pronounced Sith-Oss) is the goddess of invention and seems to particularly favour creations that take on a life of their own like clockworks or constructs, and those who create them. The sort of creative minds who engage in the sort of invention that Cythos most favours, however, are often not prone to worship or evangelization—they tend to devote themselves to invention instead. As a result, her following is not very large. Some believe that Cythos is a Gnome, while others believe she is the ultimate creation of the fallen [[Obellia|Obellian Empire]].

The Bronze Lady is a member of the [[Heroic Pantheon]], and rose to divinity during [[The Age of Calamity]].

**Edicts:** craft new creations, pay attention to details, share achievements
**Anathema:** carelessly destroy others’ creations or research, enslave intelligent constructs, abuse constructs, refuse to acknowledge or learn from mistakes
**Areas of Concern:** clockwork, invention, time
**Religious Symbol:** mask with forehead rune
**Sacred Animal:** termite
**Sacred Colour(s):** bronze, silver
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Heroic Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
Cythos doesn’t dole out punishments or rewards to her followers lightly.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Knowledge floods your mind. Once, you can reroll a failed skill check to [Recall Knowledge](https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=26); you must use the second result, even if it’s worse.

**Moderate Boon**: You become a wellspring of invention. You gain the [Inventor](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=798) feat, even if you don’t meet its prerequisites, and when you roll a [Crafting](https://2e.aonprd.com/Skills.aspx?ID=4) check for the Inventor feat, you use the result one degree of success better than the result you rolled.  

**Major Boon**: You gain the companionship of a loyal construct ally of Cythos’s choice at least 1 level lower than you. If the construct is destroyed, it rebuilds itself over the course of a week, and as you gain levels, the construct upgrades and improves itself, becoming more powerful.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Your hands shake when attempting delicate work. All your failures to [Craft](https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=43) or [Repair items](https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=42) are instead critical failures.  

**Moderate Curse**: Whenever you try to perform engineering or science tasks, everything just seems to explode. Whenever you attempt to create or use an alchemical item or a construct, [Disable a Device](https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=69), and so on, you must attempt a DC 15 flat check. On a failure, something explodes and you take 1d6 fire damage (or a different type of damage, if appropriate) per level of the item, construct, or device (DC 40 basic Reflex save).  

**Major Curse**: Constructs—even mindless constructs you thought you could control—seek your demise. All constructs are [hostile](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=23) to you and attack you when they notice your presence. Occasionally, inanimate statues and other objects animate as constructs to pursue Cythos’s vengeance.
