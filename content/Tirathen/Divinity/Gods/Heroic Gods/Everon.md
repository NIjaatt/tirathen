**Everon** (pronounced Ever-On) is the god of justice, loyalty and hard work. He is commonly depicted as a golden lion with a sun for a mane. Many of Everon's devout believe that he is the son of [[Radia]]. He was born a [[Beastfolk]], and is held in reverence by many of his kin.

The Golden Lion is a member of the [[Heroic Pantheon]], and rose to divinity during [[The Age of Calamity]].

- **Edicts:** Finish any and all tasks you accept, bring those who are cruel to justice, show pride in your home and your heritage
- **Anathema:** Break your word, be cruel to the innocent, rebuke someone due to their homeland
- **Areas of Concern:** Justice, loyalty, work
- **Religious Symbol:** Lion faced shield
- **Sacred Animal:** Lion
- **Sacred Colour(s):** Brown, gold
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Heroic Pantheon]]

Everon is known to have never once granted a boon or curse to mortals.
