**Vrakul** (pronounced V-Rah-Cool) is a chaotic god often depicted as a [[Goblin]], with massive rippling muscles and beautiful locks of golden hair. Known and worshipped as the strongest and coolest Goblin to ever exist, he instils the idea that in numbers there is strength, strength equals power, and power equals money. Many goblinoids follow his creed and rulership by creating allegiances within the different tribes and paying as much respect as possible to the chain of command. He was born a goblin, and is held in high regard by Goblins and [[Hobgoblin|Hobgoblins]].

The Big Boss is a member of the [[Heroic Pantheon]], and rose to divinity during [[The Age of Calamity]].

**Edicts:** Take what you can, fight tactically, respect your boss
**Anathema:** Bow before your lessers, let others take what is yours, permit insubordination
**Areas of Concern:** Strength, thievery, work
**Religious Symbol:** Chain and manacle
**Sacred Animal:** Cougar
**Sacred Colour(s):** Green, red
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Heroic Pantheon]]
