**Garethor** (pronounced Gareth-Ore) is the god of Bravery, Drunkards, Freedom and Luck. A natural born troublemaker, and enjoyer of drink. Known to be extremely lucky, even as a mortal. It's said he was never meant to become a god, he simply showed up drunk to his older brother's ascension and was somehow included. Yet such descriptions do Garethor disservice, for this is a god braver than even the mightiest. He does not hesitate to uplift and aid others, even at great cost to himself. He was born a [[Dwarf]], and is viewed with reverence by all Dwarves.

The Young Luck is a member of the [[Heroic Pantheon]], and rose to divinity during [[The Age of Calamity]] alongside his older brother, [[Branthor]].

- **Edicts:** drink, aid the oppressed, seek glory and adventure
- **Anathema:** waste alcohol, be mean or standoffish when drunk, oppress the vulnerable
- **Areas of Concern:** ale, bravery, freedom, and wine
- **Religious Symbol:** tankard
- **Sacred Animal:** hound
- **Sacred Colour(s):** silver, tan
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Heroic Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
Garethor sometimes hands out his blessings and communicates his ire at seemingly random opportunities based on his drunken whims. In particular, Garethor blesses the recently liberated to help secure their freedom. Garethor is also known to grant particularly trivial and harmless curses to those who disrupt revelry. These curses typically cause the disruptor to change in appearance, taking on a comical or farcical look. He is quick to lift these curses by dawn or for those who give in to the enjoyment of the evening and join the festivities.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Garethor helps you recover from nights of carousing. While you still get drunk and otherwise experience the effects of alcohol normally, you’re never hung over the next morning.

**Moderate Boon**: You share some of the bravery Garethor espoused during his ascension. When you roll a success on a saving throw against a fear effect, you get a critical success instead. If you have the [fighter](https://2e.aonprd.com/Classes.aspx?ID=35) bravery class feature, when you roll a critical failure on a save against a fear effect, you get a failure instead.

**Major Boon**: An embodiment of freedom, you break fetters with ease. At the end of each of your turns, you can end one effect that’s currently making you [immobilized](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=81) (including effects that make you immobilized by Grabbing or restraining you) as a free action.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Individuals who raise Garethor’s passing displeasure awake as if from a hard night of drinking and find that drink tastes foul.

**Moderate Curse**: Garethor has afflicted you with cowardice. Whenever you roll a critical success on a saving throw against a fear effect, you get a success instead, and each time you roll a failure on a saving throw against a fear effect, you get a critical failure instead.  

**Major Curse**: Garethor curses you to suffer in the presence of drinking, making it hard for you to bear entering any tavern. Whenever any creature drinks alcohol within 100 feet of you, you suffer all the negative effects from the alcohol (but only the alcohol) instead. This doesn’t apply if the creature gains any special effect from drinking alcohol.
