**Nyzzara** (pronounced Niz-Zara) is the goddess of illusions, tricksters, and keepers of secrets. She is also known as the "The Veiled Lady", a name taken from the clothing she is most often depicted wearing. She safeguards those who plead for secrecy, especially those in disguise for their own safety. Nyzzara employs mirrors, reflections in water, and even sometimes shadows to connect with her most faithful. She has a rivalry with [[Baelmir]], loathing the dark god’s corruption of shadows. She was born a [[Kobold]], and is held in high reverence by her kin.

The Veiled Lady is a member of the [[Heroic Pantheon]], and rose to divinity during [[The Age of Calamity]].

- **Edicts:** show the beauty in illusions, pursue the nature of truth, respect the need for secrets
- **Anathema:** let a creature permanently labour under the falsehood of your illusions, reveal a secret you have sworn to keep
- **Areas of Concern:** illusions, mysteries, reflections, secrets
- **Religious Symbol:** seven veils tied in circle
- **Sacred Animal:** coyote
- **Sacred Colour(s):** gray
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Heroic Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
The Veiled Lady abhors the use of illusion to cause harm and detests anyone who discourages or corrupts the process of truth-seeking. The goddess works actively against the faith of [[Baelmir]] and his clerics’ use of shadow.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Nyzzara prevents your enemies from pulling off your veil. Once, when a foe rolls a success on a Perception check to disbelieve your illusion, it gets a critical failure instead. Nyzzara typically grants this benefit to protect an elaborate or consequential illusory deception, and she never uses it to improve the effect of a harmful illusion.

**Moderate Boon**: You wear a veil of illusion wherever you go. You can cast [_illusory disguise_](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=159) at will as an innate divine spell.

**Major Boon**: You spread your illusions over a larger area. You can designate an area to permanently gain the effects of [_hallucinatory terrain_](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=145). All creatures within this area gain the effects of _illusory disguise_, changing their forms as you wish. You can designate a new area as a 1-minute activity, but doing so dismisses any previously designated areas.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Your hair becomes a wild variety of colors, which cannot be altered with mundane or magical means, and it somehow grows out from under any cap, scarf, or other headwear intended to disguise it. You gain a –2 status penalty to [Deception](https://2e.aonprd.com/Skills.aspx?ID=5) skill checks to [Impersonate](https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=46) anyone else.

**Moderate Curse**: Those who betray the secrets of others find their own secrets laid bare. Each person from whom you are keeping a secret immediately learns one of your secrets involving that person. This curse doesn’t give away other people’s secrets you are keeping, only your own.

**Major Curse**: Mirrors break whenever you cross their paths. You cannot be concealed by [illusion](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=92) magic of any kind (the spell automatically fails), and all creatures that see you know your true identity
