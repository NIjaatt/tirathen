**Ulgorak** (pronounced Uul-Gore-Rack) was a mighty chieftain of orcs, and the greatest leader of his kind, who united all orcs under his banner during the Age of Calamity. Through his tribe, Ulgorak controlled nearly the entirety of eastern [[Tirathen]], from the [[Iron North]], to [[Southlands]], and instituted changes to orc society that sought to increase their standing among the other powers of Tirathen. He was born an [[Orc]], and is held in great reverence by his kin as the Chief of Chiefs. Ulgorak is almost exclusively worshipped by Orcs.

The Great Uniter is a member of the [[Heroic Pantheon]], and rose to divinity during [[The Age of Calamity]].

- **Edicts:** Improve the orcs’ lot in life, obey the orcs above your station, rule the orcs below you with a strong hand
- **Anathema:** Allow orc-kind to become weak, intentionally make orcs look bad, disrupt your hierarchy without just cause
- **Areas of Concern:** Discipline, power, prosperity
- **Religious Symbol:** Grasping hand clutching a skull
- **Sacred Animal:** Crocodile
- **Sacred Colour(s):** Green, purple
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Heroic Pantheon]]
