**Sarithar** (pronounced Sari-Thar) is the god of dragons, protection and travel. The father of all dragonkind, and father of [[Kalazhar]], the Calamity Dragon.

The Waybringer is a member of the [[Celestial Pantheon]], specifically one of the Eight Makers that created a sentient creature. Sarithar created the [[Kobold]], and Dragons.

- **Edicts:** seek and destroy evil, travel the world, help others fend for themselves
- **Anathema:** fail to pursue a foe who has betrayed your mercy, attack a creature without certainty of wrongdoing
- **Areas of Concern:** leadership, peace, virtuous dragons
- **Religious Symbol:** silvery dragon above a mirrored pool
- **Sacred Animal:** none
- **Sacred Colour(s):** gold, silver
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Celestial Pantheon]]

### Devotee Benefits
- **Divine Attribute:** Wisdom or Charisma
- **Divine Font:** [heal](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=148)
- **Divine Sanctification:** must choose holy
- **Divine Skill:** [Diplomacy](https://2e.aonprd.com/Skills.aspx?ID=39)
- **Favoured Weapon:** [jaws](https://2e.aonprd.com/Weapons.aspx?ID=356) or [staff](https://2e.aonprd.com/Weapons.aspx?ID=367)
- **Domains:** [creation](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=68), [freedom](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=77), [protection](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=90), [travel](https://2e.aonprd.com/Domains.aspx?ID=93)
- **Cleric Spells:** 1st: [anticipate peril](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=2343), 4th: [creation](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1477), 5th: [summon dragon](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1697) (can summon any dragon, regardless of tradition), 9th: [falling stars](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1521)

### Divine Intercession
Sarithar is known to have never once granted a boon or curse to mortals.
