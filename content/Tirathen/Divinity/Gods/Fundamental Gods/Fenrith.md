**Fenrith** (pronounced Fen-rith) is the god of the hunt, community and farmers.

The Old Hunter is a member of the [[Celestial Pantheon]], specifically one of the Eight Makers that created a sentient creature. Fenrith created the [[Athamaru]].

**Edicts:** care for your home and family, fulfill your duties, keep the peace, protect the community
**Anathema:** abandon your home in its time of need, choose yourself over your community, tarnish your reputation, tell lies
**Areas of Concern:** family, farming, hunting, and trade
**Religious Symbol:** bow and arrow
**Sacred Animal:** stag
**Sacred Colour(s):** brown, green
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Celestial Pantheon]],

### Divine Intercession
Fenrith favours those who commit themselves to their communities and detests those who disrupt these families.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Fenrith shares in his bounty as long as you work for it. Whenever you roll a critical failure at a check to Subsist in the wild, you get a failure instead.

**Moderate Boon**: You share Fenrith’s sharp eye, allowing you to use a [longbow](https://2e.aonprd.com/Weapons.aspx?ID=436) in any situation. When you attack with a longbow, you can ignore the longbow’s [volley](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=730) trait, and longbows have double the normal [range](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=2195) increment for you.

**Major Boon**: You become a friend to the land. You can cast [speak with plants](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1681) as a divine innate spell. When you’re in a healthy natural environment, the land sustains you without need for food or drink. You can cast the [commune](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rituals.aspx?ID=114) ritual using Nature without any secondary casters or secondary checks.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Fenrith curses your table and punishes you for relying on others’ hard work. Any food you didn’t grow, gather, or otherwise harvest yourself tastes like ash and leaves you [fatigued](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=73).

**Moderate Curse**: Fenrith’s beasts take a dislike to you. Animals’ attitudes toward you are one category worse (friendly instead of helpful, indifferent instead of friendly, and so on).

**Major Curse**: Fenrith leaves you unable to fend for yourself and forces you to rely on your community. Any attempts to grow or otherwise acquire food on your own always fail. You must instead ask others for help with these matters, whether in the form of farming, hunting, or simply asking for a meal. These attempts always require a skill check (typically Diplomacy to Request) and the DC is usually a very hard DC for your level.
