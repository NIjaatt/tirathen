**Zarion** (pronounced Zar-eon) is the god of enlightenment, perfection, knowledge, healing and inner strength. Widely considered to be one of the most powerful beings in existence, yet remains neutral in matters that do not concern himself or his followers.
 
The Enlightened One is a member of the [[Celestial Pantheon]], specifically one of the Four Watchers that did not make a sentient creature. Instead choosing to oversee their siblings work.

**Edicts:** be humble; help others perfect themselves; hone your body, mind, and spirit to a more perfect state; practice discipline
**Anathema:** engage in overly unhealthy or self-destructive behaviours, destroy an important historical text, repeatedly fail to maintain self-control
**Areas of Concern:** history, knowledge, and self-perfection
**Religious Symbol:** blue hand
**Sacred Animal:** snail
**Sacred Colour(s):** blue, white
**Pantheons/Covenants:** [[Celestial Pantheon]]

### Divine Intercession
Zarion grants his boons to those making progress on their paths toward perfection. He avoids bestowing misfortune as punishment, preferring to do so only to give an individual a challenging obstacle to overcome so as to help them progress in their quest for self-perfection.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Zarion grants you great insight and knowledge. Once, when you roll a failure at a check to Recall Knowledge, you get a critical success instead. Furthermore, the check loses the secret trait, so you know for sure that the result was a critical success. Irori typically grants this boon for an extremely consequential check to Recall Knowledge.

**Moderate Boon**: Your body rebuilds after adversity, becoming stronger. You can cast [harmonize self](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=2064) as an occult qi spell; your key spellcasting attribute is Wisdom. If you didn’t have one already, you gain a focus pool with 1 Focus Point and are trained in the spell attack modifier and spell DC statistics.

**Major Boon**: You can temporarily ascend to a greater form. You can cast 8th-rank [sacred form](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=2020) once per day as an occult innate spell. When you do, your form appears unremarkable, rather than clad in powerful armaments.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Zarion challenges you to seek another path, rather than treat every problem as a nail just because you have a hammer. If you select a check for which you have a higher bonus when another method would’ve been more appropriate for the situation, such as using Deception to lie through life because it’s your highest modifier, even if it would be better to reach a compromise through Diplomacy, you must roll twice and take the lower result.

**Moderate Curse**: Zarion challenges you to adapt to adversity in order to perfect yourself. You become weighed down as if under heavy weights, becoming [clumsy](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=61) 2 and [encumbered](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=70) until you accomplish a challenging task of Irori’s choosing.

**Major Curse**: Zarion makes all living creatures forget your existence. This can be further compounded by raising Zarion’s ire, resulting in your name being obliterated from all written records. In both cases, memories and writings rearrange themselves to omit you smoothly, rather than leaving obvious gaps.
