Imprisoned since [[The Age of Creation]], the Outer God **Nhalgorath** (pronounced Naal-gore-wrath) seeks only to destroy creation and the other gods. Believed to be imprisoned in a state of torpor within [[Tirathen]]'s second moon, [[Belegyr]]. It's increasingly restless stirrings are taken by many to be the cause of frequent Lunar Eclipses as well as strange ocean tide behaviour.

Nhalgorath has many names; the Great Destroyer, the Enemy of All, the Unmaker. It is not a member of any Pantheon. For every god, Celestial, Dark and Old alike, all agree on one single thing; Nhalgorath cannot be released from it's prison.

- **Edicts:** destroy all things, free Nhalgorath from it's prison
- **Anathema:** create something new, let material ties restrain you, torture a victim or otherwise delay its destruction
- **Areas of Concern:** destruction, disaster, and wrath
- **Religious Symbol:** fanged spider
- **Sacred Animal:** None
- **Sacred Colour(s):** None
- **Pantheons/Covenants:** None

### Divine Intercession
Nhalgorath's imprisonment means it can almost never intercede on Tirathen, but yet Enemy of All's might is so powerful that the god's profane influence can leak through it's prison. It is as likely to curse those who draw it's attention as it is to reward them, though great enough acts of violence and destruction may earn it's favour.

#### Boons
**Minor Boon**: Your attack becomes more destructive. Once, one of your failed or successful attacks becomes a critical hit, and your weapon or unarmed attack gains the [deadly](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=570) d12 trait for this attack, replacing any deadly trait it already had. Nhalgorath typically grants this boon when the critical hit could destroy something precious or significant.

**Moderate Boon**: You can devour all. Your mouth grows into a horrible, circular, insectile maw of needle-like teeth. You gain a bite unarmed attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage. Additionally, you can stomach eating anything you can fit down your throat—even rocks or small objects. You gain a +4 status bonus to saves resulting from eating something, such as diseases, poisons, or other effects of the ingested object. This doesn’t allow you to digest magic items that would otherwise be difficult or impossible to destroy, such as cursed items or artifacts.

**Major Boon**: You can crack the surface of Tirathen, accelerating the release of the Enemy of All. You gain the [Quaking Stomp](https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=5879) barbarian feat; you can use this feat even when not in rage.

#### Curses
**Minor Curse**: Whenever you are imprisoned, you thrash and shake as your mind fills with desperation. Any time you are [restrained](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=90) or otherwise imprisoned (such as being sent to jail), you are also [clumsy](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=61) 2 and [stupefied](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=94) 2.

**Moderate Curse**: Your destructive rage seeks to remain unbound. Any armor you wear automatically becomes broken. Other items in your possession occasionally become broken over time.

**Major Curse**: You are useful only as a beacon of destruction. You are permanently [confused](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=63), rampaging against anything and everything in your sight, and you deal an additional 18 damage with your unarmed and weapon Strikes.
