Belegyr is the second moon of [[Tirathen]], constructed by the gods during [[The Age of Creation]] to seal the Enemy of All, [[Nhalgorath]].

Belegyr is not tidally locked, and thus freely rotates during it's orbit. From Tirathen, Belegyr is seen to have a large chuck of its surface missing. This is due to a large asteroid that crashed into Belegyr, fracturing the moon and sending massive fragments crashing down onto the lands of Tirathen in an even known as [[the Great Collapse]].

Size-wise, the moon has a surface just seven percent that of Tirathen's, much larger than it's sister satellite, [[Llunys]]. It also orbits Tirathen further away than Llunys.
