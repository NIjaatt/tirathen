At the heart of [[Karreth]], Four towers stand signifying the four spell traditions sit at the heart of the city, where mages train and study in pursuit of knowledge and power. These towers are known as the Tradition Towers.

Collectively, the magic city of Karreth views magic as a force of nature, that interacts with the fundamental laws of the world and bends them to it's will. Magic is alive, and it must be respected. However, each tower's tradition expands on these views and seeks to understand and utilize magic in their own ways.

### The Tower of Axiom
Members of the Axiom Tower are Arcane practitioners. They seek to use logic and rational thought to understand magic, pouring over tomes and grimoires to solve their magic equations.

### The Tower of Covenant
Members of the Covenant Tower are Divine practitioners. Unlike their fellow traditions, they don't seek to understand magic but hold absolute faith, and use that belief as their source of power.

### The Tower of Revelation
Members of the Revelation Tower are Occult practitioners. They seek to understand the unexplainable through magic, and utilize magic in a way different to their fellow traditions.

### The Tower of Verdance
Members of the Verdance Tower are Primal practitioners. They seek to understand magic by using an instinctual connection and faith in nature, the turning of seasons and the natural selection of life.
