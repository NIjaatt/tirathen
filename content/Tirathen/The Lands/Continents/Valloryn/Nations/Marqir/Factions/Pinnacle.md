Karreth's government is a Noocratic Magocracy, where the four leading Archmages of each [[The Tradition Towers|Tradition Tower]] elects their most intelligent, and wise candidates to ascend to the [[Pinnacle|Pinnacle Tower]]. Casting off their magical ideologies of their past towers, and governing Karreth as a neutral force as well as serving as diplomatic figureheads.

Mages who are selected to become Pinnacle Ascendants, are bound by a divine oath to Asurion to never cast magic during their ten year service. If they break this oath, they would invoke Asurion's wrath and be cursed with madness or even lose their magical abilities entirely. Any mage that wishes to become the Archmage of their prospective tower must take part in this service. Of the Pinnacle Ascendants, whomever is deemed the wisest and most intelligent through a series of elite tests, is elected to serve a one year term as the Prime Ascendant. The current Prime Ascendant is [[Tyrkon Halloway]].
