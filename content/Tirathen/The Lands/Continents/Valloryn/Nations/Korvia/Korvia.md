The Korvian Empire is an empire located in western [[Valloryn]]. Korvia is represented by a Dragon wielding a sword that carries a crown.

### Basic Info
- **Coat of Arms:** Dragon wielding a Sword that carries a Crown
- **Demonym:** Korvian
- **Location:** The [[Westlands]]
- **Capital:** [[Ashkareth]]
- **Religion:** Split. Half follow [[Azrahnism]], the other follow the [[Octavian Faith]].
- **Government:** Constitutional Monarchy
 - **Emperor:** [[Aedric Baragav II]]
 - **Marshal:** [[Dorrian Holt]]
 - **Chancellor:** [[Serios Arteria]]
 - **Regional Governors:** [[Four Ducal Houses]]

#### Formation
 + Believed to be first settled around ~400 P.C
 + Officially recognised as a sovereign nation in 1400 P.D

### History
#### Pre-Humans
Few know or even remember a time when the lands of the West and Heartlands were known as Erebos. A time when the lands belonged to the [[Elf|Elves]], a time before [[Human|Humans]]. Legend says that the Westlands were divided in two, each centred around a primordial god. The north revered [[Soloun]] of the Flame, whose fire kept them warm from the cold. While the south revered [[Utharun]] of the Earth, who blessed their land with abundance. These two gods each brought prosperity and peace to their people for many years, until one day, the gods went to war with each other, and so too did their people. War was raged for hundreds of years; but eventually the people realised the error in their ways, and reconciled. All while the gods continued to battle each other, and some say they are still fighting to the present.

#### The Arrival of Humans
At first, relations between the off-world Humans and the native peoples of Tirathen were peaceful. Humans were given permission from the Elves to remain where they arrived in [[Voryn]]. However, something else entered Tirathen alongside humans. Magic; a mysterious supernatural force that almost seems to be just as alive as any other. It spread through the lands of Tirathen, granting its boons to many. However, Magic is not native to the lands or peoples of Tirathen, their bodies not acclimated to hold such power.

[[The Spellplague]] as it became known, devasted the lands of Tirathen, but none were affected more so than the Elves, who stood on ground zero of the deadly plague. The Elves were forced to vacate their lands, and retreat to their ancestral home of [[Valeniir]], where they remained for neigh a millennia, and have only recently begun re-entering the world in the present.

#### Conquerors
As the natives of Tirathen were being wiped out by the Spellplague, Humans saw chance to capitalize on the weakness of their new home. As their bodies were already acclimated to magic, they used it in force to conquer, taking the lands of Korvia and [[Valdyr]] with ease. This mass expansionist war became known as [[the Hundred Year War]].

The Holy Kingdom of Halmyr responded, launching the [[The Holy Crusades|Second Holy Crusade]] in their history against the invaders of Tirathen. However, the campaign ultimately failed as their armies fell to the Spellplague, and despite having a mighty ground force, there was naught Halmyr could do in the face of the magic wielding humans.

The Humans were mighty, and were lead primarily lead by the Six Dragon Knights, each bonded with a different type of Dragon that accompanied them from the old world. This group was comprised of Athelmyr their leader and his Five Swords. Powerful warriors who served their liege with honour. However, as Humans spread further across the continent, and for reasons long forgotten, the fifth sword defected to the east and claimed independence from Athelmyr, forming the country of Valdyr.

It is for this reason, that the Hundred Year War is not remembered for the Humans mass expansion, but rather the brutal civil war between the fifth sword who held his own against the full might of Korvia.

#### Formation of a Nation
After many years, Athelmyr was forced to cede Valdyr to the traitorous sword. So Athelmyr looked back on their captured lands and settled at the heart of the Westlands, and named the settlement, Ashkareth which means Dragonthrone in Draconic. He gave land to each of his remaining swords for them to rule and preside over, before passing away. His legacy carried by his only son, Darian who wielded his power to grant the people power, by the formation of a government body that answered to the people. Over time, the Four Swords evolved to become the [[Four Ducal Houses]] of Korvia.

As the empire grew and generations passed. The workload of the Emperor became too much for a single man to complete. So the Emperor of the time, Jaycel II decided to grant authority to his two closet aides. He named one, the Chancellor and granted them authority over the Imperial Senate and ministries. The other, he named the Marshal, and granted them authority over the Imperial Armies and Four Ducal Houses.

#### [[The War of the Lions]]
The War of the Lions was a conflict between the Korvian Empire and the Kingdom of Valdyr that lasted 20 years, starting with the Valdyrian Offensive in 1320 A.C, and ending with the settlement of an uneasy peace in 1341 A.C.

In 1340, both monarchs had retreated from the front. With Emperor [[Aedric Baragav II|Aedric II]] in [[Ashkareth]], and Thalagar in [[Eryndor]]. The Emperor had been dealing with numerous rebellions over the years during the War, which he had always dealt with easily. However, during the summer of 1340 one of his own Ducal houses attempted a coup. Garran Valcryn of House Valcryn, Duke of Thars, when ordered to make a retreat from Thars by Marshal Darrian Holt, had marched his army all the way to the capital and demanded more military support.

Such actions, disobeying his Marshal and marching on his own Emperor proved to be a terrible decision. For the Chancellor, Serios Arteria had predicted such an outcome, and advised the Emperor to mobilize his Imperial Guard, which were waiting for the Duke. Garran Valcryn, his firstborn son Garran II, and the generals of his army were promptly executed. However, House Valcryn was allowed to continue existing, and was succeeded by Garran's youngest son Alric, at just ten years of age. The Imperial Guard then rallied with Kalaen's Ducal Forces and retook Thars. As this was happening, the [[The Great Northern Exodus|Great Northern Exodus]] was ongoing, straining Aedric II's abilities.

With Thalagar facing intense political stress and turmoil back home, they simultaneously came to an agreement to cease the war in 1341 A.C.

### Geography
Korvia is located in western Valloryn. It is bordered to the southeast by the [[Marqir|Shifting Sands of Marqir]], to the northeast Valdyr, finally to the north lies the [[Free Tribes]].

The great Westwall Range runs along the eastern edge of Korvia's lands, shielding the empire from Marqir and Valdyr except for two passes. Korvia's coast is predominately the Dividing Sea. However the region of Voryn in the far south borders the Torrid Ocean.

Korvia features a wide range of climates and terrains due to its large size. Generally, the climate of the empire is temperate, with some parts of the Northern territories having a much colder climate, and the south being much warmer.

Many mountain ranges dot the lands of Korvia, especially in the southern regions. There are also a number of vast forests that Korvia hosts, the largest of which is the Great Giliath Forest.

### Government & Politics
Korvia's government functions as a constitutional monarchy. Power is divided between the Emperor and his Royal Family, the Marshal who leads the empire's armies and commands the regional lords of the Four Ducal Houses, and the Chancellor who leads the Imperial Government, which is composed of the Imperial Senate and Imperial Supreme Court.

**Emperor**: The Emperor is the head of state and the highest authority in Korvia. Despite the Empire being a constitutional monarchy, the Emperor still wields a substantial amount of power in the ability to make executive orders and decisions, and can choose to overwrite any executive decision made by either the Marshal or the Chancellor. The Baragav Family is the ruling dynasty of the Empire, and has been since it's inception. The current emperor is Aedric Heimdyr Baragav II. The royal family resides at Scaled Palace in the imperial capital, Ashkareth. The emperor lives here with his wife, Empress Priscilla, and three children: Prince Lukiel, Princess Eylin, and Crown Prince Eisen.

+ **Imperial Guard:** An elite standing army sworn directly to the Royal Family. It operates independently of the Marshal's command and serves both as the Emperor's personal army and a strategic reserve during wartime.

**Imperial Government:** The highest position of the imperial government is the position of Chancellor. The Chancellor is the only other person besides the Emperor that possesses the power to make grand political decisions. The Chancellor manages the Imperial Government. The Chancellor plays a crucial role in shaping the empire’s domestic and foreign policies as they also must serve as a representative for the empire in other nations. Currently the position of Chancellor is held by Serios Arteria. With executive power, the Chancellor has power over many government organizations such as Ministry of Justice, Ministry of Finance, as well as the Imperial Housing Agency and the Food Agency.

+ **Imperial Senate**: Serves as the imperial governments parliamentary body. The Senate participates in legislative processes and advise the Emperor and Chancellor. The Senate functions as a bicameral legislature, consisting of representatives from each province and major city. Comprising the Senate are the House of Representatives; led by the [[Civic Faction]], and the House of Founders, led by the [[Noble Faction]].

+ **Imperial Supreme Court**: Serves as the judiciary body of the imperial government. The court has the power to determine sentences for crimes and invalidate legislation from the Senate as well as executive actions from the Emperor or Chancellor.

**Imperial Army:** The highest position of the imperial army is the position of Marshal. The Marshal is the only other person besides the Emperor that possess the power to make grand military decisions. The Marshal manages the Imperial Army, as well as the regional armies of the Four Ducal Houses. The Marshal however, cannot directly command the Ducal armies outside of their borders outside of wartime. During peaceful times, any orders given by the Marshal to any one of the Dukes is a strong suggestion at best. However, given the military prowess and mind required of the rightfully earned title of Marshal, such suggestions are typically heeded. Currently the position of Marshal is held by Dorian Holt.

+ **[[Four Ducal Houses]]:** Each region in Korvia, aside from the capital region of Orahen; has a regional lord from the Four Ducal Houses, each with their own territory and responsibilities. For the most part, they each oversee the local administration, taxation, and law enforcement of their respective territory through their Regional Armies.

#### International Relations
Korvia has a only a few countries that touch with its borders, and the empire cares little for the world beyond their neighbours, aside from the [[Halmyr|Holy Kingdom of Halmyr]] which is the seat of their primary faith.

Famously, Korvia and Valdyr are frequently at war with one another fuelled by an ancient, long forgotten grudge.

Finally, the Empire cares naught for the Free Tribes within the [[Frozen North]]. Korvia views their land as inhospitable and long since decided not to annex the region. Recently, a larger and larger number of northerners have been trying to enter Korvia in an event known as [[the Great Northern Exodus]]. Korvia allowed the northers into the country at first, but as the numbers grew higher and higher, Korvia decided to shut down the border.

### Economy
Korvia's economy can be divided into three main categories: mining, agriculture, and foreign trade.

#### Mining
The eastern edge of the Empire is dotted with a significant amount of natural mineral resources. Iron is by far Korvia's greatest economical power, which is used for manufacturing weapons, armour, and buildings.

#### Agriculture
Kalaen is often referred to as the "region of growth". It is called so, for it is where recruits to the Imperial Army are sent to train, and it's the location of a vast number of schools. However, the true reason for this name is the vast farms that supply Korvia with food.

#### Foreign Trade
The most important products for export are Korvia's mineral resources. Which other nations, especially the [[Dunvaar|Dunvaar Commonwealth]] pay good coin for.

### Demographics
The majority of the Korvian populace are Humans. However, the demographics of Korvia strongly reflect the rarity seen in the [[Ancestry Overview]] document. With Dwarves, Gnomes, Goblins, Halflings, Kobolds and Orcs all being commonly seen people here. Beastfolk are also present in great number, however are more often than not slaves.
