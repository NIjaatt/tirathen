The Emperor is the head of state and the highest authority in [[Korvia]]. Despite the Empire being a constitutional monarchy, the Emperor still wields a substantial amount of power in the ability to make executive orders and decisions, and can choose to overwrite any executive decision made by either the Marshal or the Chancellor. The Baragav Family is the ruling dynasty of the Empire, and has been since it's inception. The current emperor is Aedric Heimdyr Baragav II. The royal family resides at Scaled Palace in the imperial capital, Ashkareth. The emperor lives here with his wife, Empress Priscilla, and three children: Prince Lukiel, Princess Eylin, and Crown Prince Eisen.

+ **Imperial Guard:** An elite standing army sworn directly to the Royal Family. It operates independently of the Marshal's command and serves both as the Emperor's personal army and a strategic reserve during wartime.
