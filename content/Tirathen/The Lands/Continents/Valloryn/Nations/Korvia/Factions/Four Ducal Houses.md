The Four Ducal Houses are [[Korvia|Korvia's]] four most powerful noble families. Each House upholds the highest of noble traditions and influence over the Imperial regions entrusted to them by the Emperor.

### House Valcryn
House Valcryn controls the region of [[Thars]]. It's previous head was Garran Valcryn, who was executed for treason. It's current head is his youngest son, Alric Valcryn.

### House Cassovar
House Cassovar controls the region of [[Kalaen]]. It's previous head was Seredan Cassovar, who is too old to lead anymore. It's current acting head is his daughter, the Duchess Maelira Cassovar.

### House Aerthain
House Aerthain controls the region of [[Relion]]. It's previous head was Othram Aerthain, who passed away in 1343 A.C. He was succeeded as head by his eldest son, Pelivar Aerthain.

### House Belthir
House Belthir controls the region of [[Voryn]]. It's previous head was Halvar Belthir, who retired several years ago. It's current head is his son, Lucain Belthir.
