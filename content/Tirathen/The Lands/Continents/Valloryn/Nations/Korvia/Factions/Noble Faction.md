The Noble Faction is a faction of [[Korvia|Korvian]] Politics, led by Duke Belthir which seeks to uphold the status quo in Korvian politics and upper-class society.

### Background
The Noble Faction one of the two major conflicting sides in the Imperial Senate, challenged only by the increasing power of the [[Civic Faction]], led by Chancellor [[Serios Arteria]]. The powerful tax reforms and expanding influence of the Civic Faction, and consequent reduction of power of the Nobility of Korvia, is greatly upsetting and angering the nobles of Korvia.

### Members
Duke Belthir leads the faction, with Duke Cassovar serving as his second in command. A large number of smaller noble houses are a part the noble faction.
