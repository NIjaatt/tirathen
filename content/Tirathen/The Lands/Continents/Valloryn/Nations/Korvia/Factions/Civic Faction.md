The Civic Faction is a faction of [[Korvia|Korvian]] Politics, led by Chancellor [[Serios Arteria]]. It is a political faction that directly challenges the political power and ideologies of the [[Noble Faction]] who have long since held power.

### Background
The Civic Faction was founded under the guidance of Chancellor Serios Arteria. It primarily consists of high-ranking Korvians who are either commoners by birth or nobles who share the same progressive values as them. The faction believes that the Noble traditions of Korvia are long since outdated, and must be discarded in order to usher in a new age of greatness.

Under the Civic Faction, Korvia has seen a seen significant tax reforms that has weakened the Noble Faction, and redistributed that wealth to the people. They have increased Military funding, as well as the establishment of the Intelligence Division, a branch of the Imperial Army dedicated to spycraft and collection of information.

### Members
Chancellor Serios Arteria leads the faction with a small team of his trusted associates.  A large number of merchants, small noble houses, and esteemed commoners who all share progressive values, make up the civic faction.
