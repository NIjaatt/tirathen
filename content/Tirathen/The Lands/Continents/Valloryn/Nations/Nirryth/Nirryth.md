### Nirryth
The isle of Nirryth knew no permanent inhabitants before the Athamaru arrived on Nirryth in 1337 A.C, pushed southwest by the naval forces of the [[Fourfold Dominion]] following their [[Rising of the Athamaru|rising]] from the Emerald Sea. Eight years on, the communities of Nirryth are still in the early work of becoming something. Shelters have been built. Crops are being attempted with varying success. The domesticated eels that Athamaru communities rely on have proven surprisingly adaptable to the brackish coastal waterways of the isle. The shape of a life on land is taking form, however imperfect.

### Geography
Nirryth is an island located off the south-western coast of Valloryn, separated from the mainland by ocean and seas.

The island features a few forests. Nirryth's climate ranges is tropical. A small mountain range runs along centre side of the island.

### Government & Politics
Nirryth is not a nation in any conventional sense. There is no crown, no charter, no unified government. What exists on Nirryth is a loose scatter of Athamaru communities, each self-governing according to their own traditions, cooperating with their neighbours out of goodwill and shared necessity rather than any binding political structure. That Nirryth appears on maps and in diplomatic correspondence at all is largely a matter of convenience; outsiders needed something to call it, and the name of the isle was what stuck.

The various communities of Nirryth, despite their cooperation are divided. Their displacement from the ocean has cracked open a question that every community's matriarch must now answer: is this isle a refuge or a home? One current of thought holds that Nirryth is temporary, and that the ocean remains the Athamaru's rightful world, and that the communities on the isle should keep themselves ready to return to it when circumstances allow. The other holds that the ocean is gone in any meaningful sense, that whatever it is that awakened in the deep sea is not going anywhere, and that the future lies in learning to live on land properly rather than in half-measures.

Communities that hold opposing views remain on cordial terms, trading freely and sharing warnings of danger as Athamaru custom demands, but the underlying tension is real. What Nirryth becomes in the decades ahead will depend largely on which of these two convictions proves more persuasive.

#### International Relations
The Fourfold Dominion, for its part, pays Nirryth little attention. The isle offers the pirates nothing of obvious value, and the Athamaru are careful not to give them reason to look twice. Fishing parties venture into the shallows and coastal waters quietly, never straying far enough to invite confrontation. It is an arrangement built entirely on mutual disinterest, and both sides seem content to leave it that way.
