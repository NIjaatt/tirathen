House Myarthyn has spent the Long Mourning doing what [[Elf|Elves]] do best: becoming extraordinarily good at something specific. In their case, that something is craft; textiles, metalwork, perfumery, and the kind of meticulous artisanal production that has made them the wealthiest house on [[Valeniir]] by a considerable margin. They have also, during this time, built an understanding of trade and material value that no other house comes close to matching.

Their vote for re-engagement was the one that made the majority possible, and both factions know it. Myarthyn did not join the re-engagement faction out of ideology but because they believe isolation is, among other things, an economic condition. They have a clear sense of what Valeniir's craftsmanship could be worth to a world that has never seen it. House Myarthyn is the swing vote, and everyone on the [[High Council of Valeniir|council]] knows it. The current head of House Myarthyn is Veylis Myarthyn.
