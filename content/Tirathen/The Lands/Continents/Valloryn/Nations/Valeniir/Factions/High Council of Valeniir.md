The High Council of Valeniir is composed of the five oldest noble houses of Valeniir, each holding a single seat. No house holds formal precedence over another, and all votes are equal. The weight a house carries in any given debate is a product of its reputation, its history, and the personal authority of whoever speaks for it on a given day.

The current Council is defined by a three-to-two split between the re-engagement faction and the isolationist faction.
