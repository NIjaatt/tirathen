House Venthryn occupies an interesting position on the [[High Council of Valeniir|Council]]. Their senior members lived through the tail end of the Long Mourning and remember the isolation as a choice that once made sense. Granting them perspective, and a pragmatism the other re-engagement houses lack. It is their belief that [[Valeniir]] has been standing still far too long while the world has moved forward. Valeniir cannot afford to stand still any longer.

House Venthryn has a long history of producing diplomats and negotiators. A skillset that went largely unused during the Long Mourning and is now, for the first time in centuries, relevant again. There is a quiet hunger in Venthryn that has nothing to do with conquest and everything to do with purpose restored. The current head of the House is Sorel Venthryn.
