### History
#### Pre-Humans
In the early days of Halmyr's existence, it was known by another name. Though now lost to time. Many scholars believe Halmyr to be the original home of the fallen [[Obellia|Obellian Empire]]. Though such claims are often revoked by the Octavian Faith. Halmyr's story, at least what is known, begins with the formation of the Octavian Faith.

#### Formation of Nation
The first appearance of the Octavian Faith is said to be in the during [[The Age of Calamity]]. In this tumultuous time, the Faith provided the populace, who feared for their lives, a path to peace and comfort. Via faith. One of their notable actions during this time period was dispatching members all across the continent, to endorse various future rulers. Allowing them to later build their cathedrals and convert nations to their religion.

Yet the founders of the religion needed a land to call home. They looked to the east, where a land lay in ruins. There, they settled and named the lands Halmyr, and began forming a Holy Kingdom.

In the centuries after, its influence continued to spread, resulting in it becoming the dominant religion in Tirathen. Eventually, not even nobles and rulers could ignore its power anymore.

#### [[The Holy Crusades]]
The Holy Kingdom of Halmyr have proven themselves to have an aversion to violence and a preference for peace, yet have equally proven themselves to act if they must. Three times in Tirathen's history has Halmyr acted in such a way.

The First Crusade took place to fight back a Demonic invasion that destroyed and corrupted the Kingdom of Taldar. The Second Crusade took place when the Humans stole Elven Lands and poisoned the lands with their magic. The Third and most recent Crusade took place to fight against the Lich King, Tyr Al'Bahir.

### Geography
Halmyr is located in eastern Valloryn. It is bordered to the southwest by the [[Marqir|Shifting Sands of Marqir]], to the northeast [[Myrathin]], to the north lies the [[Badlands|Badlands of Taldar]], and finally to the south lies [[Valeniir|The Lands of Valeniir]].

At the heart of the Holy Kingdom lies the great mountain, Brightspire. Home to the capital of Halmyr, Sanctum Octavum. To the east, the Norspine Mountains shield Halmyr from the [[Marqir|Shifting Sands of Marqir]]. To the north, the Crescent Range separates Halmyr from Myrathin.

To the northeast lies one of the few [[World Tree|World Tree's]] left standing. It's existence sacred and protected. It marks the border to the Badlands of Taldar, where massive pillars known as the [[Gates of Ikanos]] are embed into the earth to seal back the demonic corruption of [[the Darkrift]].

Halmyr's climate is temperate, with some parts of the Northern territories having a much colder climate, and the south being much warmer.

Many mountain ranges dot the lands of Halmyr and there are also a number of vast forests that Halmyr hosts. Halmyr is also home to one of few [[World Tree|World Tree's]] left standing on Tirathen.

### Government & Politics
Halmyr is where the High Seat of the Octavian Faith is established. The kingdom is ruled by the Pope of the Octavian Faith.

The Holy Kingdom of Halmyr is divided into three main cities. [[Celmia]], [[Dawnkeep]], and Sanctum Octavum. These three cities act as branches of the Octavian Faith. Celmia is home to the [[Ministry of Gospel]]. Dawnkeep, the [[Ministry of Prosecution]]. Lastly, Sanctum Octavum is home to the Pope and their [[Iscariot Guard]]. There are many pilgrims and clergymen that travel between these three main cities, with these three being a gathering place for believers and church officials alike.

The Pope oversees governance of not only the Octavian Faith but also the Holy Kingdom. However, the majority of governance is made up of various administrative organizations that handle specific tasks, giving it an almost state-like structure. The Pope sees command over one other Ministry, being the [[Ministry of Providence]]. A hidden and secretive group of spies and intelligence operatives that work and answer directly to the Pope.

#### International Relations
As the homeland of the Octavian Faith, Halmyr retains a special kind of authority when it comes to international relations. The nation has an important political role, recognising states, kingdoms and empires around the continent as sovereign nations in their own right. 

### Economy
Halmyr's economy is perhaps the strongest across all the lands of [[Tirathen]]. Various donations across the lands see a constant supply of foreign gold and minerals in the form of jewellery and coin. These are often melted down and repurposed into official Halmyran coins, imprinted with the likeness of the current Pope of the era to prove authenticity. For this reason, Halmyran coins are the most traded currency in the world.

Many pilgrims and believers that travel from far and wide are willing to pay significant sums to tread the holy grounds of their forebears. Church visits, witnessing holy artefacts, etc.

### Demographics
The demographics of Halmyr strongly reflect the rarity seen in the [[Ancestry Overview]] document. With Beastfolk, Dwarves, Gnomes, Halflings and Humans being the most commonly seen ancestries. Other ancestries, both common and of greater rarity are also present here, but are in limited number.
