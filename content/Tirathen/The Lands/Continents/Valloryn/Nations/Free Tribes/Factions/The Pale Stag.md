The Pale Stag are a tribe from the eastern mountains of the [[Frozen North]].

### The Tribe
The Pale Stag are a tribe whose traditions are deeply rooted in the [[Old Faith]], specifically in the belief that great decisions should not be made without a sign from the world itself. To the Pale Stag, acting without omen is how people make the kinds of mistakes that cannot be undone.

They cooperate with the [[Free Tribes]] coalition freely, sharing resources and warnings without hesitation, and they have no ill will toward [[Valdris]] personally. They simply have not received a sign that this unity is the correct path, and until they do, they will not formally commit.

#### Leadership
Their spiritual leader and chief are the same person, an elderly woman called Serath Dun, who says very little and observes a great deal.
