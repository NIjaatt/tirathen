The Redtusk are a tribe from the wastelands of the [[Frozen North]].

### The Tribe
The Redtusk are a racially mixed tribe whose origins lie in a generations-old merging of multiple smaller groups that would have otherwise died out separately. They carry no shame about this. Their identity was built from the moment of that joining, and they have spent generations proving that a tribe defined by pragmatism and loyalty is as durable as one defined by blood.

They were among the first to follow [[Valdris]], not because he is Orcish, but because the logic he put forward was the same logic they had already been living. When [[the Iron Howl]]and the Redtusk formally aligned, it gave the [[Free Tribes]] its first real backbone.

#### Leadership
Their current chief is a [[Human]] man named Orra Veld. He is one of the few tribal leaders who speaks to Valdris as an equal rather than a supplicant.
