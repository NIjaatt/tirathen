The Iron Howl are an Orcish tribe from the deep tundra interior, far enough north that even by the standards of the [[Frozen North ]]they were considered hard people.

### The Tribe
They built their identity around endurance rather than conquest, the belief that surviving what should kill you is its own form of glory. Their traditions involve scarification rather than trophy-taking, marking what a warrior has survived rather than what they have killed. This made them something of an outlier even before the freeze, admired but not widely liked, a tribe more respected than befriended.

#### Leadership
The Iron Howl was [[Valdris|Valdris's]] tribe, before he rose to his current station as leader of the [[Free Tribes]]. His authority was not, and continues to be the kind that makes people feel warm and steadied. The Iron Howl are currently led in Valdris's absence by his older sister Graka, a veteran hunter whose patience with the other coalition chieftains is notably shorter than her brother's.
