The Stonefang are a tribe from the western forests of the [[Frozen North]].

### The Tribe
The Stonefang are an Orcish tribe who value strength and leadership above all. Every member of the tribe actively compete for the role of chief, and it is easily taken. They fight frequently with other tribes, and have won land, people and food by might many times. Forced by the freeze, they journeyed south to the refugee camp outside of [[Korvia|Korvia's border]].

The Stonefang follow the [[Free Tribes]] coalition directives when it suits them and drag their feet when it does not. They believe their own chieftain is more suited to the role of High Chieftain, being older, and stronger than [[Valdris]]. That Valdris unified the camps first is, in the Stonefangs's view, a matter of timing rather than merit, and timing is not the same as worthiness.

#### Leadership
The Stonefang are led by an Orc named Harruck, who does bot believe Valdris is wrong in his beliefs so much as he believes Valdris is in the wrong position. Harruck is older, more decorated, and by the traditional measures of the [[Frozen North]], more credentialed than the High Chieftain. He led his people through the worst years of the freeze without losing a single elder. He won three inter-tribal disputes before the coalition existed. By every metric he was raised to value, he is the better candidate. Harruck has never moved against Valdris openly, and likely never will. He is a hunter first and foremost, so he waits.
