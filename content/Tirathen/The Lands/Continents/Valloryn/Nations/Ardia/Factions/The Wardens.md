The Wardens are perhaps the most well-regarded mercenary company in [[Ardia]], and one of the few that has earned genuine respect from the Halmyran administration rather than merely their tolerance.

#### Foundation
Originally a faction within [[the Steel Fist]], when their leader Garrosh died without naming a successor, the company fractured. This faction branched off and created their own mercenary company, naming themselves the Wardens. They focused on protection work, escort contracts, and the occasional recovery of people taken by the criminal networks that thrive in Ardia's ungoverned stretches. They charge fair rates and are known to negotiate reduced fees for clients who cannot afford their standard contract.

#### Present Day
The Wardens are currently lead by Sera Dunnvash, a scarred veteran. She runs the Wardens with a maternal firmness, and has turned down more contracts than most companies ever receive, but her people know exactly why. The Wardens do not take work that leaves people worse off than they found it.
