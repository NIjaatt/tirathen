The Steel Fist are the common ancestor of [[The Wardens]], and [[the Red Scorpion]] mercenary companies. It was the first mercenary company of [[Ardia]], comprised of Ardia's ex-military and patriots of Ardia.

#### Foundation
This mercenary company was founded by Orcs in the aftermath of Halmyr's colonisation. These Orcs refused to leave Ardia but equally refused to serve the new Halmyran government. So Ardia's ex-military chose a third path, mercenary work. A call was put out and all of Ardia's Orcish military gathered under one commander whose name is still spoken with reverence in certain taverns, Garrosh. When he died without formally naming a successor, the company fractured.

The Wardens and the Red Scorpion both trace their origins to that split, inheriting different pieces of the old Steel Fist's philosophy. The what remained of the Steel Fist occupies the middle ground between them: rigorous, professional, and entirely indifferent to the moral weight of a contract so long as it is clearly defined and properly paid. They have fought wars on the side of justice and injustice in the same season without contradiction to their values, and see no hypocrisy in it.

#### Present Day
The Steel Fist are currently lead by Commander Gorvan. He is a blunt, methodical man who genuinely believes that an Orc who picks sides is an Orc who has let feeling compromise discipline.
