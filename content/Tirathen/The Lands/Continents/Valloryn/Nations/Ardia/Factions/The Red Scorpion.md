The Wardens are perhaps the disliked mercenary company in [[Ardia]]. They are considered fiends, outlaws and murderers. Yet none can dispute their usefulness and willingness to perform any job, for the right coin.

#### Foundation
Originally a faction within [[the Steel Fist]], when their leader Garrosh died without naming a successor, the company fractured. This faction branched off and created their own mercenary company, naming themselves the Red Scorpion. They operate entirely within the letter of Orcish contract tradition, never breaking a sworn agreement, never lying about what they will do. What they will do, however, is considerable. The Red Scorpion takes assassination contracts, enforcement work for the criminal networks operating out of Ardia's port towns, and occasional work for collectors stealing priceless artefacts from governments and organisations.

#### Present Day
The Red Scorpion are currently lead by Dask Morruven. He is charming in a way that unsettles people once they realise it. He is deeply proud of the company's unblemished contractual record, and speaks of it the way a craftsman speaks of fine work.
