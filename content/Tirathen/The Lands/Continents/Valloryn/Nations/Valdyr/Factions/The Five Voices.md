The King of Valdyr rules alone, but tradition demands they hear the advice of the Five Voices. An advisory council that is defined by function, they are needed to keep the realm alive.

### The Voice of War
The Voice of War is a role filled by a Knight. They oversee and advise the king on matters of war, military doctrine, and battlefield strategy. The current Voice of War is Vaelric Thorne.

### The Voice of Coin
The Voice of Coin is a role filled by a Merchant. They oversee and advise the king on matters of economy, supply, trade, and fundings. The current Voice of Coin is Maeran Keln.

### The Voice of Magic
The Voice of Magic is a role filled by a Mage. They oversee and advise the king on matters of the arcane, magical logistics, and arcane warfare. The current Voice of Magic is Iltharyn Vos.

### The Voice of People
The Voice of People is a role filled by a Spymaster. They oversee and advise the king on matters of intelligence gathering, morale, dissent, and internal stability. The current Voice of People is Serah Vayne.

### The Voice of Gods
The Voice of Gods is a role filled by a Priest. They oversee and advise the king on matters of divine will, omens, oaths and morality. The current Voice of Gods is Caldrim Aessos.
