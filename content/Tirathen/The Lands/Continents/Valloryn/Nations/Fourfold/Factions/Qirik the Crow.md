Qirik the Crow is one of the Sea Lords of the [[Fourfold Dominion]]. A Tengu of middling years and boundless patience, Qirik's reputation rests on knowing things he shouldn't.

### The Jackdaw
Ask any sailor on the Emerald Sea who they would least like to see on the horizon and a surprising number will say Qirik the Crow, despite the Jackdaw being one of the smaller Sea Lord vessels.

The Jackdaw's crew move quietly, talk to everyone, and leave ports with far more than they paid for. Qirik maintains what he calls a network of interested parties across every significant harbour in Valloryn, a web of dock workers, innkeepers and bored harbour officials who pass information along in exchange for the Dominion's tolerance of their own quiet arrangements.

In the Emerald War, Qirik's contribution was intelligence rather than firepower, and many believe it was his knowledge of the other Lords' positions and timing that made Amir's final victory possible.
