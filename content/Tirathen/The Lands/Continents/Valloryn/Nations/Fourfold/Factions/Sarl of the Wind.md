Sarl of the Wind is one of the Sea Lords of the [[Fourfold Dominion]]. A Lizardfolk of few words and considerable stillness, Sarl reads water and weather in a way that borders on the supernatural, and his crew have learned to trust that stillness absolutely.

### The Undercurrent
Sarl of the Wind is the oldest of the Sea Lords by a wide margin, and the Undercurrent is a ship that has been rebuilt so many times around the same keel that Sarl jokes the only original timber left is the one he stood on when he first took command. When Sarl goes quiet on the deck, something is coming. When he smiles, it is already over.

The Undercurrent's defining legend is the passage of the Splitrock Narrows in 1340 A.C, a strait between the [[Isle of Nirryth]] and [[Korvia]], widely considered impassable in storm season. Yet Sarl threaded the strait at night, running dark, to deliver a cargo worth more than most nations' annual trade. No other captain has attempted it since.
