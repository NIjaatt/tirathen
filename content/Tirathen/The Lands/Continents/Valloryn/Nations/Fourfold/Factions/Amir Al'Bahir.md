Amir Al'Bahir is the Pirate King of the [[Fourfold Dominion]]. The sole victor of [[the Emerald War]] between the Four Lords of the Emerald Sea.

Amir wears his name like a blade pointed outward. Where other Al'Bahir's hunched and apologised, he made the name a promise.

### The Revenant
The Revenant is the most feared ship on the Emerald Sea, and Amir Al'Bahir intends to keep it that way. She is a massive, dark-hulled galleon, running no flag but the Fourfold Dominion's diamond, and she moves like she owns every stretch of water she sails.

His crew are drawn from every corner of [[Valloryn]], outcasts and veterans and people who have run out of places to be unwelcome. All of them loyal to a man who has never once pretended the world owed him anything.

The Revenant's greatest deed came during the Emerald War itself, when Amir's crew fought a running three-day engagement against the combined fleets of Lohtala and Qirik and emerged victorious without losing a single man. It was that battle, more than anything else, that convinced the other Sea Lords to bend the knee.
