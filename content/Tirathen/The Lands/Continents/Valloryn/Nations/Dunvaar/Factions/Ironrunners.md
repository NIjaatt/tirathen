Beneath the Jovr and Thegin of [[Dunvaar]] sits the Faction Council, the body through which the daily life of every Dunvaaran is governed. The Council is composed of the elected heads of each of Dunvaar's crafting factions, of which there are many, each representing a distinct discipline of industry and invention. Factions govern their own affairs independently, managing resources, setting craft standards, and resolving internal disputes without interference from the dual rulers. Of the numerous factions, three are dominant political forces above all others, the [[Stonebloods]], the [[Sparkwelds]], and the Ironrunners.

#### The Ironrunner Faction
The Ironrunner factions sits nestled between the Stoneblood and Sparkweld factions in terms of political influence. Responsible for the design, construction, and maintenance of the train networks connecting the holds. Young by Dunvaaran standards, the Ironrunner's rise to dominance was with simple, unavoidable necessity. Every hold relies on their rails. Every faction depends on their deliveries. Nobody in Dunvaar is eager to make an enemy of the people who control how goods move through the Commonwealth, and the Ironrunner's have never needed to say so aloud. The Ironrunner faction is led by Torvin Geldrak Ironrunner.
