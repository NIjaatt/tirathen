Beneath the Jovr and Thegin of [[Dunvaar]] sits the Faction Council, the body through which the daily life of every Dunvaaran is governed. The Council is composed of the elected heads of each of Dunvaar's crafting factions, of which there are many, each representing a distinct discipline of industry and invention. Factions govern their own affairs independently, managing resources, setting craft standards, and resolving internal disputes without interference from the dual rulers. Of the numerous factions, three are dominant political forces above all others, the [[Stonebloods]], the Sparkwelds, and the [[Ironrunners]].

#### The Sparkweld Faction
Predominantly Gnomish, and perpetually chaotic in the eyes of outsiders. Their faction are inventors, and tinkerers, whose leaders have historically viewed the Stonebloods reverence for tradition as the single greatest obstacle to Dunvaaran progress. These two factions have been rivals for as long as either can remember, their relationship one of grudging mutual dependence dressed up as contempt. The Sparkweld faction is led by Widdervane "Wid" Crellwick Sparkweld.
