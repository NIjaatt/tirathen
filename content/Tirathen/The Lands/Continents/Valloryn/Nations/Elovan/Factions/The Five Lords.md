Elovan is governed under a binding legal framework commonly referred to as he Compact which establishes shared sovereignty between Lewys Rathven and a council of five ruling officials collectively known as the Five Lords.

### The Lord Protector
The Lord Protector serves as the military and diplomatic head of Elovan, charged with the defence of the principality and the execution of foreign policy.

The current Lord Protector is [[Lewys Rathven]], who founded the nation and divided his political power among his fellow lords.

### The Lord of Coin
This seat controls taxation, trade policy, and state expenditure. No sustained military action or major civic undertaking may proceed without its assent.

The current Lord of Coin is Cassyr Danovar, a prominent merchant who leads the Danovar company.

### The Lord of Herladry
Representing the landed nobility, this seat oversees feudal obligations, land charters, and the traditions of nobility.

The current Lord of Heraldry is Ser Mattis Corvayne, a noble house that once served Valdyr but defected alongside Lewys.

### The Lord of Courts
The legal cornerstone of Elovan, this seat interprets the Compact, supervises courts, and adjudicates disputes between the Lords themselves.

The current Lord of Courts is Soren Valyce, a justice from the [[Halmyr|Holy Kingdom of Halmyr]].

### The Lord of Guilds
This seat represents urban centres, guilds, and skilled labourers. It oversees supply, production, and logistics.

The current Lord of Guilds is Hadrik Dewfall, a dwarven engineer famed for his designs.
