**Myrathin's Blood, Myrathin's Future**

Led by Dara Harrian.

#### Core Identity
The Patriot Party was founded in the decades after the revolution by veterans of the republican militias who felt that the constitution had been too generous and focused on abstract equality and not enough on rewarding those who had actually bled to build the republic. Over a century that instinct has evolved, calcified, and in some corners, curdled.

#### Voter Base
The Patriot Party's strongest base is rural and small-town Beastfolk. Communities that feel economically left behind and culturally overlooked by the Union Party's urban focus. The party also draws from military and veteran communities across all subspecies, and has a significant if uncomfortable relationship with more extreme Beastfolk nationalist groups that it officially disavows and quietly embraces.

#### Policy Pillars
The Patriot Party's platform rests on four broad commitments.

**Economic Nationalism:** The Patriot's protectionist trade policy prioritizes Myrathi industry and workers. They hold a scepticism of foreign investment.

**Military Strength:** The Patriot's boast a very robust defence budget, and a hawkish attitude toward Myrathin's neighbours, particularly those who once enslaved their people.

**Beastfolk Heritage:** The Patriot's cultural policies that centre on Beastfolk identity specifically rather than the Union's more inclusive framing, with rhetoric that frequently emphasizes what Beastfolk sacrificed to build the republic.

**Law and Order:** The Patriot's historically have strong institutions, and enforce harsh penalties for crime, creating a complicated relationship with the very nationalist violence their rhetoric often inspires.

#### Internal Factions
The Patriot Party is not monolithic, and is made up of three key sub-factions:

The Traditionalists are Harrian's nominal base. They are fiscally conservative, militaristic, institutionally republican but Beastfolk-first in their cultural outlook. They are uncomfortable with the party's drift toward extremism but unwilling to challenge Harrian directly as she is their strongest voice and leader in recent history.

The Nationalist Wing is the faction that gives everyone else pause. They are openly supremacist in sentiment if rarely in explicit policy language. They draw on the very real generational trauma of Beastfolk history and redirect it toward something uglier. They are the fastest growing faction in the party.

Lastly, the Reform Conservatives are a shrinking minority. Made up of patriot voters and members who want the party's economic competence without its nationalist drift. Several have defected to the Union Party in recent cycles.

#### Core Vulnerability
The Union Party's great weakness is it's corruption. Several scandals over the last two decades have shown a consistent patterns. Military contracts, infrastructure spending, and back-channel dealings with foreign interests that sit awkwardly against their nationalist rhetoric.
