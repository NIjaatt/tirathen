Valloryn is a continent of [[Tirathen]], and the most heavily settled lands. Valloryn possess as many varied cultures and peoples, and an ancient history stretching back many thousands of years.

The continent is home to once-great empires and kingdoms such like the [[Obellia|Obellian Empire]],  the [[Taldar|Kingdom of Taldar]], and the [[Marqir|Marqiri Union]], as well as the rising powers of the Korvian Empire and the Holy Kingdom of Halmyr.

Valloryn is roughly estimated to be 1,409,490 km².

### Nations
The lands of Valloryn play host to a number of nations, who all play a part in the formation of Valloryn's future.

#### [[Korvia|The Korvian Empire]]
A vast, militaristic nation in western Valloryn, known for its powerful military and aristocratic traditions.

#### [[Valdyr|The Kingdom of Valdyr]]
A militant kingdom in north-central Valloryn, known for it's powerful military and frequent bouts with Korvia despite it's small size.

#### [[Elovan|The Elovanite Principality]]
A recently formed, and contested principality in north-central Valloryn, known for it's complete ideological rejection of it's neighbouring kingdom, Valdyr.

#### [[Free Tribes|The Free Tribes]]
A coalition of the Frozen North's many peoples, united under a single banner by desperation and a growing conviction as they seek to escape a slow, wintry death.

#### [[Karreth|The City State of Karreth]]
The nation of magic lies in south-central Valloryn, and is known for its advanced magical studies, commitment to diplomacy, and wealth of natural resources.

#### [[Ardia|The Ardian Protectorate]]
A colonial protectorate state governed by Halmyran appointed officials, known for being where the Lich King is sealed away and for being the home of some of the most renowned mercenary groups in Valloryn.

#### [[Halmyr|The Holy Kingdom of Halmyr]]
A theocratic kingdom in eastern Valloryn, and the home of the [[Octavian Faith]].

#### [[Myrathin|The Republic of Myrathin]]
A large, democratic republic in north-eastern Valloryn, known for it's diverse population of [[Beastfolk]].

#### [[Dunvaar|The Dunvaaran Commonwealth]]
A commonwealth located in north-eastern Valloryn, renowned for its advanced technology and incredible craftsmanship.

#### [[Fourfold Dominion|The Fourfold Dominion]]
A nation of pirates ruled by the Pirate King, and the legendary pirates lords beneath him.

#### [[Nirryth]]
The isle of Nirryth is home to the Athamaru and their people, having fled the oceans to make the surface their home.

#### [[Valeniir]]
The ancestral homeland of the reclusive Elves. A nation shrouded in mystery and unknowns by the mainland nations and people.

### Mountains
Several mountain ranges segment western and central, north and south, as well as east and central Valloryn into its broader regions. There also exists many ranges outside of these great divides. The most well known ranges are;

- The **Norspine Mountains**, that separate the central Heartlands from the Shifting Sands, running along the centre of the continent.
- The **Westwall Range**, they separates the Westlands from the Heartlands and the Shifting Sands, running from the southernmost point of Valloryn, up into the Frozen North.
- The **Red Mountains**, this range dots the southern end of the Shifting Sands. Isolating the Southlands.
- The **Grey Mountains**, they mark the realm of the Dunvaar Commonwealth.
- The **Crescent Range**, this range marks the centre of the eastern Valloryn, and separates a number of different nations.

### Bodies of Water
#### Rivers
Countless rivers and waterways are etched across its lands, connecting many of its nations and people through trade and travel.

#### Lakes
Many inland lakes also cover the temperate continent. However, the most famous lake is the massive **Lake Aroden**, nestled around the vast ranges of the Norspine Mountains. Long believed to be a holy place, where the gods themselves first set foot on Tirathen.
