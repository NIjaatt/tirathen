The Westlands of Valloryn is home to the [[Human]] Nation of [[Korvia]], but was originally the lands of a nation of [[Elf|Elves]] long since forgotten by all, except the Elves.

### Region Details
Host Nation: [[Korvia]]
Area Estimate: ~216,000 km²
Travel Time (Longest Distance): 30 days walking (25ft speed).
