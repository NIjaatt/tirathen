Brenvar is the central region of the [[Heartlands]]. It is the capital region of the [[Valdyr|Kingdom of Valdyr]]. To it's west lies the region of [[Tharros]], to the south lies [[Thryss]], and to it's east lies the capital region of the [[Elovan|Elovanite Principality]], [[Dornach]].
