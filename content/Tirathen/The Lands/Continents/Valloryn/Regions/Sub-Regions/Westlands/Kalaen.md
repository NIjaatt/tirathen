Kalaen is the central-north region of the [[Westlands]]. To it's north, lies the region of [[Thars]], and to it's south lies the capital region of the [[Korvia|Korvian Empire]], [[Orahen]].

Kalaen is managed and led by one of the [[Four Ducal Houses]] of Korvia. The House Cassovar.
