Relion is the central-south region of the [[Westlands]]. To it's north, lies the capital region of the [[Korvia|Korvian Empire]], [[Orahen]], and to it's south lies [[Voryn]]. Relion also borders the [[Marqir|Shifting Sands of Marqir]] to the east.

Relion is managed and led by one of the [[Four Ducal Houses]] of Korvia. The House Aerthain.

### Geography
Relion lies to the south of the beating heart of the Korvian Empire. To it's west, lies the Dividing Sea.

The region holds a strong strategic standpoint, as it borders with Marqir, the nation of magic. If such a force were ever to invade from Marqir, a defensive line could swiftly be employed before an eventual counterattack. Due to the strategic importance of the region, the north-eastern area has a constant military presence by Korvia's military.

Generally, the climate of Relion is temperate. With some parts of the southern areas of the region having a much more equatorial climate. 

Relion also plays host to small mountain range to it's west, named Dragonclaw Range. To the east, lies the Giliath Forest, which is the largest forest in Korvia.
