Voryn is the southernmost region of the [[Westlands]], and it's southernmost region of the [[Korvia|Korvian Empire]]. To it's north, lies the region of [[Relion]], to it's south and west the Torrid Ocean, and the Dividing Sea.

Voryn is managed and led by one of the [[Four Ducal Houses]] of Korvia. The House Belthir.

### Geography
Voryn is the southern peninsula of the Westlands, and the southernmost point on the mainland of [[Valloryn]].

Generally, the climate of the region is consistently hot, humid and wet, for it lies close to the equator. With some parts of the northern areas of the region having a much more temperate climate.

Voryn is home to the Grayborne Mountains that begins at it's north, slicing down and to the west, before circling back east and ending about a days travel from the southern coast.
