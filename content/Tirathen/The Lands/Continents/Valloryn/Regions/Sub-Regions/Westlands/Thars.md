Thars is the northernmost region of the [[Westlands]]. To it's south, lies the region of [[Kalaen]]. To the north, Thars borders the [[Frozen North]], and to the east, [[Valdyr]].

Thars is managed and led by one of the [[Four Ducal Houses]] of Korvia. The House Valcryn.
