Orahen is the central region of the [[Westlands]]. It is the capital region of the [[Korvia|Korvian Empire]]. To it's north lies the region of [[Kalaen]], and to the south lies [[Relion]].

Orahen is jointly managed and led by the Emperor and his Royal Family as well as the Chancellor and their government bodies.

### Geography
Orahen is the beating heart of the Korvian Empire, and home of the capital of Korvia,  Dragon's Thone.

The region holds a strong strategic standpoint. As it's able to swiftly respond, and dispense military action to it's north and south with ease.

Generally, the climate of Relion is temperate. With some parts of the southern areas of the region having a much more equatorial climate. 

Relion also plays host to small mountain range to it's west, named Dragonclaw Range. To the east, lies the Great Giliath Forest, which is the largest forest in Korvia.
