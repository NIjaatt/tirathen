The Shifting Sands of Valloryn is home to the fallen nation of [[Marqir]]. Once a thriving desert nation, when [[the Spellplague]] spread through the lands, the sands of Marqir came alive. Burying the nation beneath the Shifting Sands. Within the region, lies a single city, magically stabilised and floating above the sands, [[Karreth]] the city of mages.
