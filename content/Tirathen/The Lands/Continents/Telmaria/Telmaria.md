Telmaria is a continent of [[Tirathen]], located to the west of [[Valloryn]], across the Dividing Sea. 

At one time, central Telmaria was the heart of civilisation and advancement. It was a major territory of the giants, who once ruled all of Tirathen long before the rise of mortals. Their Empires since perished and fell to obscurity.

Today, the lands of Telmaria are mostly comprised of [[Beastfolk]]. However, it remains a dangerous and wild place. Whether one is exploring the unmapped jungles of [[Jurai]], traversing the northern lands of [[Arkhen]], or braving the great desert of [[Elsaim]], there are many ways to die throughout Telmaria.
