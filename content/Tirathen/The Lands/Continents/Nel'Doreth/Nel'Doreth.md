Nel'Doreth is a continent easily forgotten. It lies between the continents of [[Telmaria]] and [[Valloryn]].

The great tides of the Dividing Sea sometimes cover this land entirely, hiding the continent for decades at a time. It has recently resurfaced once again.

It's appearance is often seen as a ill omen, with the island being the birthplace of [[the Dooms of Tirathen]].
